Final Reward

LORE ITEM NO DROP

Required to zone into Veeshan's Peak from the Skyfire Mountains zone line. The Key of Veeshan is added to your key ring upon use and is permanent and reusable.

Overview

Veeshan's Peak is the fortress of the Ring of Scale, home to some of the most powerful dragons on Kunark. To enter the zone you must possess the Key of Veeshan, obtained by completing a lengthy quest chain with Emperor Ganak in Trakanon's Teeth. The quest requires you to assemble the three lost medallions of the extinct Iksar tribes (Obulus, Jarsath, and Kylong) and bring proof of Trakanon's death.

Each medallion must be reassembled from three scattered pieces by locating the correct NPC historian. Once all three complete medallions and Trakanon's Tooth are in hand, you turn them all in to Emperor Ganak to receive the key.

Medallion of the Obulus

Assembling the Medallion — NPC: Slixin Klex (Burning Woods)

▶

Reward: Medallion of the Obulus

Turn in all 3 pieces to Slixin Klex in Burning Woods. Hail him and say "medallion", then "pieces" to learn the locations.

Obtain the Bottom Piece — pained soul in Emerald Jungle / Trakanon's Teeth
▶

The Piece of a Medallion (Bottom shard of the Obulus Medallion) drops from a pained soul (Level 43) that roams Emerald Jungle and Trakanon's Teeth. The pained soul is an undead iksar ghost.

Obtain the Middle Piece — a rotting skeleton in Trakanon's Teeth
▶

The Piece of a Medallion (Middle shard of the Obulus Medallion) drops from a rotting skeleton in Trakanon's Teeth. Slixin Klex's dialogue mentions this piece is held by "an iksar ghost in Trakanons."

Obtain the Top Piece — trade Burnished Wooden Stave to Ssolet Dnaas in Warsliks Woods
▶

The Piece of a Medallion (Top shard of the Obulus Medallion) is obtained from Ssolet Dnaas (Level 50) in Warsliks Woods. Hail him and say "search" to learn he wants a Burnished Wooden Stave.

The Burnished Wooden Stave is a ground spawn in Burning Woods on a crate near the giant fort.

/map -425.85 1866.57 BurnishedWoodenStave

Hand the Stave to Ssolet Dnaas and he will give you the Top Piece.

Ssolet Dnaas says, 'Sssss. Thisss is what I need. You have my thanksss. Please, take thiss. I know not what it iss for but maybe you will find a ussse for it.'

Turn in all 3 pieces to Slixin Klex
▶

Give all three Pieces of a Medallion (items 19956, 19957, 19958) to Slixin Klex in Burning Woods.

Slixin Klex says, 'Ssssss. I thought I would never see these. I never thought the symbol of our people would be made whole again. I feel no rest for my sssoul though. My torment still flows as freely as when my peoples first suffered fiery death. I need this not. It is useless to me. Here, you take it. Maybe you can find some good to do with it.'

You receive: Medallion of the Obulus.

Medallion of the Jarsath

Assembling the Medallion — NPC: Xiblin Fizzlebik (Timorous Deep)

▶

Reward: Medallion of the Jarsath

Turn in all 3 pieces to Xiblin Fizzlebik in Timorous Deep. Hail him and say "looking for", then "help" to learn the locations. He mentions the pieces were likely hidden in water regions.

Obtain the Bottom Piece — a bloodgill marauder in Lake of Ill Omen
▶

The Piece of a Medallion (Bottom shard of the Jarsath Medallion) drops from a bloodgill marauder (Level 40) in Lake of Ill Omen. Bloodgill marauders are goblin-type NPCs found around the lake.

Obtain the Middle Piece — an ancient Jarsath in Firiona Vie
▶

The Piece of a Medallion (Middle shard of the Jarsath Medallion) drops from an ancient Jarsath (Level 42) in Firiona Vie. The ancient Jarsath is a named undead iksar that can be found near the ruins area of the zone.

Obtain the Top Piece — ground spawn in the Dreadlands
▶

The Piece of a Medallion (Top shard of the Jarsath Medallion) is a ground spawn in the Dreadlands. It spawns on the ground near some ruins in the zone.

/map 2935 50 JarsathMedallionPiece

Turn in all 3 pieces to Xiblin Fizzlebik
▶

Give all three Pieces of a Medallion (items 19959, 19960, 19961) to Xiblin Fizzlebik in Timorous Deep.

Xiblin Fizzlebik tinkers with the three pieces and snaps the pieces together. 'Wow.. You've done it.. but I suddenly feel very.. strange.. This medallion is cursed! Here, you take it!'

You receive: Medallion of the Jarsath.

Medallion of the Kylong

Assembling the Medallion — NPC: Professor Akabao (Lake of Ill Omen)

▶

Reward: Medallion of the Kylong

Turn in all 3 pieces to Professor Akabao in Lake of Ill Omen. Hail him and say "tribes", then "kylong", then "medallion" to learn the locations. He mentions the pieces are hidden beneath fortresses that are centers of Iksar and Sarnak necromancy.

Obtain the Bottom Piece — ground spawn in Lake of Ill Omen
▶

The Piece of a Medallion (Bottom shard of the Kylong Medallion) is a ground spawn in Lake of Ill Omen. It spawns underwater in the lake.

/map -444 57 KylongMedallionPiece

Obtain the Middle Piece — Verix Kylox's Remains in Karnor's Castle
▶

The Piece of a Medallion (Middle shard of the Kylong Medallion) drops from Verix Kylox's Remains (Level 52) in Karnor's Castle. This is a named NPC found deep inside the castle. He is on a long respawn timer, so be prepared to camp or check back.

Obtain the Top Piece — trade gems to Niblek in Chardok
▶

The Piece of a Medallion (Top shard of the Kylong Medallion) is obtained from Niblek (Level 10) in Chardok. He is a small goblin NPC who likes shiny things.

Hail Niblek and say "treasure", then "jewelry". He asks for a Black Sapphire and a Ruby.

Niblek says, 'Niblek likes shiny tings very much! You bring Niblek two shiny tings! Niblek has seen black sapphire and ruby! Niblek likes them very much!'

Hand him both gems. He gives you the Top Piece.

Niblek says, 'I much happy now!! I want shiny tings for long time! I give you ugly broken necklace now!'

Turn in all 3 pieces to Professor Akabao
▶

Give all three Pieces of a Medallion (items 19962, 19963, 19964) to Professor Akabao in Lake of Ill Omen.

Professor Akabao says, 'For ages this medallion has been scattered across these inhospitable lands, I had just about given up hope of locating the pieces and rebuilding this piece of Iksar history. You may keep it. It is enough to know that I have done my part to restore such an artifact to the people of Norrath.'

You receive: Medallion of the Kylong.

Trakanon's Tooth

Slaying Trakanon — Old Sebilis

▶
Obtain the Seb Key (Trakanon Idol) to enter Old Sebilis
▶

Before you can kill Trakanon, you need access to Old Sebilis. If you do not already have the Trakanon Idol, see the Seb Key guide for how to obtain it.

Kill Trakanon in Old Sebilis — loot Trakanon's Tooth
▶

Trakanon's Tooth is a 100% drop from Trakanon (Level 60), the poison dragon boss of Old Sebilis. Trakanon is a raid-level encounter requiring a full raid force. He resides deep within the dungeon.

Trakanon's Tooth is MAGIC LORE NO DROP, so every member of the raid who needs a VP key will need their own kill.

Final Turn-in

Obtaining the Key of Veeshan — NPC: Emperor Ganak

▶

Reward: Key of Veeshan (No Drop — required to zone into Veeshan's Peak)

Speak to Emperor Ganak in Trakanon's Teeth
▶

Locate Emperor Ganak in Trakanon's Teeth. He is a level 60 ghostly Iksar NPC who roams the zone. Hail him and say "Veeshan's Peak" to learn about the key. Say "key to the chambers" and then "task" to hear the full requirements.

Emperor Ganak says, 'I am not so quick to grant someone the key to their death! If you truly wish your doom to fall upon you so soon then bring me the three medallions of the fallen Iksar tribes, Kylong, Obulus, and Jarsath. The tribes were long ago crushed by the warriors of the Kunzar and their remains lie buried beneath ages of rubble. Finding the lost medallions of my people will not be enough to prove yourself worthy of entry into the fortress of the great wurms -- there is another task that you must complete before you return the medallions.'

Emperor Ganak says, 'Those who enter Veeshans Peak uninvited by the lords of the Ring of Scale become nothing more than a snack for the wurms that dwell within its halls. Once you cross the threshold of the Peak there is no escape. Any fool dedicated enough to locate the medallions can do so, but it takes a truly mighty individual to slay the dragon that has plagued my people for ages. I speak of the poison dragon Trakanon, the vile undead wurm that has conquered my peoples greatest wurmslayers. Bring me proof of Trakanons death with the medallions I request and I may deem you worthy of the key to the Ring of Scale.'

Give all 4 items to Emperor Ganak — receive Key of Veeshan
▶

Hand the following four items to Emperor Ganak at the same time:

  • Trakanon's Tooth
  • Medallion of the Obulus
  • Medallion of the Jarsath
  • Medallion of the Kylong

Emperor Ganak says, 'I must say, I thought your flesh would be rotting from your bones alongside the remains of the ancient Sebilisians. I commend you on your victory over the poison dragon and thank you for returning the ancient medallions of my people. I reward you with the key you seek. A place shall be prepared for your souls among the most honored of the dead. Soon you will be seeing the world through the shroud of death.'

You receive: Key of Veeshan.

Enter Veeshan's Peak
▶

With the Key of Veeshan on your person, travel to Skyfire Mountains and use the zone-in to Veeshan's Peak. The key is added to your key ring upon first use, so you will not need to carry it in the future.

Tips & Notes

  • The three medallion sub-quests can be completed in any order and by multiple people simultaneously. The only part that requires a raid force is killing Trakanon in Old Sebilis for his tooth.
  • Emperor Ganak is level 60 and does not aggro. He is a quest NPC only. He also handles the Seb Key quest (a much simpler quest). Do not confuse the two by saying "medallions" instead of "key to the chambers" — the medallion dialogue leads to the Seb Key quest, not the VP key quest.
  • Trakanon's Tooth is No Drop, so each person who needs the VP key must be present for a Trakanon kill and loot their own tooth.
  • The Burnished Wooden Stave ground spawn in Burning Woods has a respawn timer. If it is not there, you may need to wait or check back. Note that Ssolet Dnaas in Warsliks Woods requires non-KOS Iksar faction (at least indifferent) to accept the trade.
  • Niblek in Chardok is easy to miss — he is a small goblin hidden in the tunnels. The Black Sapphire and Ruby he wants are common gems that can be purchased from jewelers or found as drops throughout the world.
  • Verix Kylox's Remains in Karnor's Castle is a named NPC that spawns deep in the dungeon. He can be a contested camp.
  • The Kylong Bottom Piece ground spawn in Lake of Ill Omen is underwater. Use an enduring breath effect or potion to make the search easier.
  • The Key of Veeshan is added to your key ring upon first use, so you do not need to carry it in your inventory after the first entry.
  • Veeshan's Peak contains some of the most powerful dragons in Kunark, including Silverwing, Phara Dar, Druushk, Nexona, Xygoz, and Hoshkar. Come prepared with a full raid force.
