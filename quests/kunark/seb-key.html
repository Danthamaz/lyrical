Overview

Old Sebilis (Ruins of Sebilis) is the premier dungeon of Kunark, home to powerful undead Iksar and the dragon Trakanon himself. To enter the zone you must possess the Trakanon Idol, obtained by completing a turn-in quest with Emperor Ganak in Trakanon's Teeth. The quest involves killing froglok foragers and froglok hunters in the zone, looting two medallions representing the ancient Iksar tribes, and giving them to the Emperor.

The Trakanon Idol is No Drop and is added to your key ring upon use. Once you have it you may enter Old Sebilis freely on any future visit.

Step-by-Step Guide

Obtaining the Trakanon Idol — NPC: Emperor Ganak

▶

Reward: Trakanon Idol (No Drop — required to zone into Old Sebilis)

Tradeskills: None — NPC turn-in only

Items to Farm
  • Medallion of the Kunzar — 100% drop from a froglok forager
  • Medallion of the Nathsar — 100% drop from a froglok hunter
Travel to Trakanon's Teeth and locate Emperor Ganak
▶

Emperor Ganak is a level 60 ghostly Iksar NPC who roams Trakanon's Teeth. Hail him to begin the dialogue.

Emperor Ganak says, 'Greetings..It has been long since I have been aware on this plane.. Who are you and what do you want of I, Ganak, slayer of Jaled-Dar?'

Say "Sebilis" to learn about entering Old Sebilis.

Emperor Ganak says, 'An adventurer that wishes to enter the inner city of Sebilis you say?? There are only two ways in, either the [glowing orb] in the heart of the ruins in the outer city, or via one of the tortured souls that wanders the jungles nearby.'

Say "glowing orb" to learn about the idol.

Emperor Ganak says, 'The glowing orb is activated by a special key, an idol crafted in the likeness of the dread Trakanon, Reaver of Sebilis. I have this idol and will give it to you, but only if you give me two [medallions] representing the Iksar tribes of old.'

Say "medallions" to learn what he needs.

Emperor Ganak says, 'There were five different tribes of my people. The Kunzar, Kylong, Nathsar, Obulus, and Jarsath. Each of these tribes had a medallion that represented it. If you can return to me the Medallion of the Kunzar and Nathsar, I will give you the idol of Trakanon.'

Kill a froglok forager — loot the Medallion of the Kunzar
▶

The Medallion of the Kunzar is a 100% drop from a froglok forager (level 45) in Trakanon's Teeth. Multiple foragers spawn throughout the zone. The medallion is MAGIC LORE NO DROP.

Kill a froglok hunter — loot the Medallion of the Nathsar
▶

The Medallion of the Nathsar is a 100% drop from a froglok hunter (level 45) in Trakanon's Teeth. Multiple hunters spawn throughout the zone. The medallion is MAGIC LORE NO DROP.

Give both medallions to Emperor Ganak
▶

Hand the Medallion of the Kunzar and the Medallion of the Nathsar to Emperor Ganak at the same time.

Emperor Ganak says, 'You have returned to me two medallions of my people. In return, I give you this key to enter the ruins of Sebilis. Be wary though, for Trakanon, Scourge of the Sibilisian empire resides within.'

You receive the Trakanon Idol and experience.

Enter Old Sebilis
▶

With the Trakanon Idol on your person, click on the Sebilis zone-in portal in Trakanon's Teeth. The idol is added to your key ring upon first use, so you will not need to carry it in the future.

Tips & Notes

  • Both medallions drop at 100% rate, so the quest mainly depends on finding and killing one froglok forager and one froglok hunter. The frogloks are level 45 and multiple of each type spawn throughout the zone.
  • Emperor Ganak is level 60 and does not aggro. He is a quest NPC only and cannot be attacked.
  • Emperor Ganak also handles the Veeshan's Peak key quest (a separate, much harder quest). If you say "key to the chambers" or "task" he will describe the VP key requirements, which include killing Trakanon and collecting three additional tribal medallions (Medallion of the Kylong, Medallion of the Obulus, and Medallion of the Jarsath) plus Trakanon's Tooth for the Key of Veeshan. Do not confuse the two quests.
  • The Trakanon Idol is added to your key ring, so you do not need to carry it in your inventory after the first use.
  • Do not confuse this quest with the Key to Charasis (Howling Stones key), which is a separate quest obtained in the Swamp of No Hope.
  • Once inside Old Sebilis, the zone contains Trakanon himself, whose Trakanon's Tooth is required for the VP key quest.
