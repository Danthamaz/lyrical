Overview

Old Sebilis is the premier dungeon of Kunark, home to powerful undead Iksar and the dragon Trakanon himself. To enter the zone you must possess the Trakanon Idol, obtained by completing a turn-in quest with Emperor Ganak in Trakanon's Teeth. The quest involves killing froglok foragers and hunters in the zone, looting two medallions representing the ancient Iksar tribes, and giving them to the Emperor.

The Trakanon Idol is No Drop and is added to your key ring upon use. Once you have it you may enter Sebilis freely on any future visit.

Step-by-Step Guide

Obtaining the Trakanon Idol — NPC: Emperor Ganak

▶

Reward: Trakanon Idol (No Drop — required to zone into Old Sebilis)

Tradeskills: None — NPC turn-in only

Travel to Trakanon's Teeth and locate Emperor Ganak
▶

Emperor Ganak is a level 60 ghostly Iksar NPC who roams Trakanon's Teeth. Hail him and say "Sebilis" to learn about entering Old Sebilis. He will tell you there are two ways in: the glowing orb in the ruins, or via one of the tortured souls in the jungle. Say "glowing orb" and then "medallions" to learn what he needs.

Kill a froglok forager — loot the Medallion of the Kunzar
▶

The Medallion of the Kunzar is a 100% drop from a froglok forager (level 45) in Trakanon's Teeth. Multiple foragers spawn throughout the zone. The medallion is MAGIC LORE NO DROP.

Kill a froglok hunter — loot the Medallion of the Nathsar
▶

The Medallion of the Nathsar is a 100% drop from a froglok hunter (level 45) in Trakanon's Teeth. Multiple hunters spawn throughout the zone. The medallion is MAGIC LORE NO DROP.

Give both medallions to Emperor Ganak
▶

Hand the Medallion of the Kunzar and the Medallion of the Nathsar to Emperor Ganak at the same time. He will say: "You have returned to me two medallions of my people. In return, I give you this key to enter the ruins of Sebilis. Be wary though, for Trakanon, Scourge of the Sibilisian empire resides within."

You will receive the Trakanon Idol and some experience.

Enter Old Sebilis
▶

With the Trakanon Idol on your person, click on the Sebilis zone-in portal in Trakanon's Teeth. The idol is added to your key ring upon first use, so you will not need to carry it in the future.

Tips & Notes

  • Both medallions drop at 100% rate, so the quest mainly depends on finding and killing one froglok forager and one froglok hunter. The frogloks are level 45 and multiple of each spawn throughout the zone.
  • Emperor Ganak is level 60 and does not aggro. He is a quest NPC only and cannot be attacked.
  • Emperor Ganak also handles the Veeshan's Peak key quest (a separate, much harder quest). If you say "key to the chambers" or "task" he will describe the VP key requirements, which include killing Trakanon and collecting five tribal medallions. Do not confuse the two quests.
  • The Trakanon Idol is added to your key ring, so you do not need to carry it in your inventory after the first use.
  • Do not confuse this quest with the Key to Charasis (Howling Stones key), which is a separate quest obtained from Dugroz in the Swamp of No Hope and requires a Jade Chokidai Prod and Xalgozian Fang.
  • Once inside Sebilis, the zone contains Trakanon himself, whose tooth (Trakanon's Tooth) is required for the VP key quest and drops from the dragon boss inside.
