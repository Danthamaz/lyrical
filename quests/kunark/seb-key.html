Sebilis Key (Trakanon's Teeth Key)

How to obtain the key to enter Old Sebilis from Trakanon's Teeth

Overview

Old Sebilis is the premier dungeon of Kunark, home to powerful undead Iksar and the dragon Trakanon himself. To enter the zone you must possess the Sebilite Key, obtained by completing a short turn-in quest in Trakanon's Teeth. The quest involves collecting Jade Chokidai Prod pieces and turning them in to a froglok NPC near the Sebilis entrance.

The key is No Drop and must be on your person (not in a bag) to zone in. Once you have it you may enter Sebilis freely on any future visit.

Key Items

  • Sebilite Key — the final reward, No Drop, required to zone into Old Sebilis
  • Jade Chokidai Prod — dropped by Frenzied Pumas and other creatures in Trakanon's Teeth; three prods combine into a Jade Chokidai Prod Hilt
  • Jade Chokidai Prod Hilt — the partial piece turned in to begin the final stage

Key NPCs

  • a froglok shin lord — patrols near the Sebilis zone-in entrance in Trakanon's Teeth; initiates the quest when you hand over the Jade Chokidai Prod Hilt
  • a froglok knight — additional froglok guards near the entrance; may also be involved in dialogue

Step-by-Step Guide

Travel to Trakanon's Teeth and locate the Sebilis entrance
▶

Old Sebilis is located in the southeast corner of Trakanon's Teeth. The zone-in is at the base of a large ruined structure. A froglok NPC patrols this area — you need to find and speak with them to begin the quest.

Farm 3x Jade Chokidai Prod from creatures in Trakanon's Teeth
▶

Jade Chokidai Prods drop from a variety of creatures in Trakanon's Teeth, including frenzied pumas, Iksar bandits, and chokidai. Each piece is Lore, so collect them one at a time or have groupmates hold extras. You need three prods total.

Tip: Frenzied pumas near the zone border from Firiona Vie are a reliable and accessible source early on.

Combine 3x Jade Chokidai Prod to create the Jade Chokidai Prod Hilt
▶

Once you have all three prods, combine them in your inventory (no container needed — the combine is automatic when you give them to the froglok NPC). Some versions of the quest require placing them into a combining device first; check with the froglok shin lord for the exact method on your server.

Give the Jade Chokidai Prod Hilt to the froglok shin lord
▶

Hand the completed hilt to the froglok shin lord near the Sebilis entrance. He will take it and give you the Sebilite Key in return. Make sure the key is equipped or in an accessible inventory slot before attempting to zone in.

Enter Old Sebilis
▶

With the Sebilite Key on your person, click on the Sebilis zone-in portal. The key must be in your inventory (not in a bag) to pass through. You will be transported into the ruins of Old Sebilis.

Note: The key is consumed on first entry on some server versions. Verify with your server's rules — on most live-like servers the key is permanent.

✓ Progress saved in your browser

Tips & Notes

  • Trakanon's Teeth can be dangerous even before entering Sebilis — travel carefully and bring a group for farming the prod pieces.
  • The entire quest can be completed in a single session if the prods are farmed efficiently; many players complete it during a group leveling session in Trakanon's Teeth.
  • Once inside Sebilis, the zone is split into multiple sub-areas accessed through additional doors. Some of those inner areas also require keys dropped from bosses inside the dungeon.
  • Trakanon, the final boss of Old Sebilis, is required for the Bard Epic 1.0 and several other class epic quests.
