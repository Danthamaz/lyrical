Vah Shir Cloak Quests

Complete guide to Shar Vahl cloak progression for all five classes

Overview

The Shar Vahl cloak quest is a multi-tier progression for Vah Shir characters. Each of the five classes — Jharin (Bard), Dar Khura (Shaman), Khala Dun (Warrior), Taruun (Rogue), and Khati Sha (Beastlord) — has its own quest line progressing through Initiate → Recruit → Apprentice → Journeyman → Garrison cloaks.

All five Journeyman lines converge on crafting a Sonic Wolf Bane weapon using tomes from Arms Historian Qua.

Shared Prerequisite: Citizenship

All five classes must complete the citizenship quest before beginning their class-specific cloak progression. This quest grants you your Initiate's Cloak.

Receive Guild Summons from your class guildmaster
▶

Speak with your class guildmaster in Shar Vahl to begin the citizenship process. They will provide you with a Guild Summons to start the chain.

Receive Application for Citizenship and deliver it to Registrar Bindarah
▶

Your guildmaster hands you an Application for Citizenship. Take it to Registrar Bindarah in Shar Vahl to proceed with the registration process.

Get Certificate of Taxability stamped by Tax Collector Khugra
▶

Registrar Bindarah gives you a Certificate of Taxability. Take it to Tax Collector Khugra, who will return a Stamped Certificate of Taxability.

Obtain an Acrylia Slate from Mignah
▶

Find Mignah and have her create an Acrylia Slate for you. This slate will accompany your application throughout the process.

Return Stamped Certificate + Acrylia Slate to Registrar Bindarah
▶

Give both the Stamped Certificate of Taxability and the Acrylia Slate to Registrar Bindarah. She will provide you with a Note to King Raja.

Give Note to King Raja Kerrath
▶

Present the Note to King Raja to King Raja Kerrath. He will provide you with a Note from King Raja in return.

Return Note from King Raja to Registrar Bindarah
▶

Bring the Note from King Raja back to Registrar Bindarah. She will give you a Notarized Application and an Acrylia Slate of Shar Vahl.

Give Notarized Application + Acrylia Slate to your Guildmaster
▶

Turn in the Notarized Application and the Acrylia Slate of Shar Vahl to your class guildmaster. You will receive your Initiate's Cloak and have the Acrylia Slate returned to you.

Note: Registrar Bindarah can replace lost items at various stages. Guildmasters can replace a lost Initiate's Cloak.

Jharin (Bard)

Recruit Cloak — NPC: Gherik Alarhun

▶
Items to Farm
  • 3x Scorpion Shell
  • 4x Grimling Femur Bone
  • Xakra Worm Bile
  • Flask of Water
  • Quality Firing Sheet
Give Acrylia Slate to Gherik Alarhun
▶

Hand over your Acrylia Slate to Gherik Alarhun. He will task you with collecting 3 scorpion shells.

Turn in 3 scorpion shells → craft the clay bowl
▶

Turn in the 3 shells to receive a Claymixing Bowl and a Bowl Sketch (pottery craft). Grind 4 grimling femur bones to make bone powder. Combine the powder with water and xakra worm bile to make clay. Then use the pottery wheel to combine clay + Bowl Sketch + flask of water to create an Unfired Bowl.

Fire the bowl and gather drum materials from Broker Fahaar
▶

Turn in the Unfired Bowl to have it fired in a kiln using water and a quality firing sheet. Then show your Acrylia Slate to Broker Fahaar to receive the drum materials: a Stretched Hopperhide and Leather Cord.

Turn in Bowl + Initiate's Cloak + drum materials → Cloak of the Jharin Recruit
▶

Give Gherik the Bowl of the Jharin, your Initiate's Cloak, the Stretched Hopperhide, and the Leather Cord. You will receive the Cloak of the Jharin Recruit, a Drum, and a Buckler.

Apprentice Cloak — NPC: Hymnist Gawfed

▶
Items to Farm
  • 7x Owlbear Feather
Show Buckler of the Jharin Recruit to Hymnist Gawfed
▶

Present your Buckler of the Jharin Recruit to Hymnist Gawfed. He will give you Gawfed's Receipt and send you to Jihli Mahej.

Complete vase errand chain with Jihli Mahej → Large Cloth Sack
▶

Work through the vase errand chain culminating in a Finished Vase Replica. This rewards you with a Large Cloth Sack.

Fill sack with 7 owlbear feathers + Recruit Cloak → Bag of Feathers
▶

Place 7 owlbear feathers and your Recruit Cloak into the Large Cloth Sack to create a Bag of Feathers.

Complete lute string chain: Gawfed → Jangle → Gawfed → Jangle → Bag of Lute Strings
▶

Gawfed gives you a Note for Jangle. Deliver it to Jangle, who returns a Note from Jangle. Take that back to Gawfed to receive Gawfed's Request. Return Gawfed's Request to Jangle to finally receive the Bag of Lute Strings.

Turn in Bag of Lute Strings → Cloak of the Jharin Apprentice + Lute
▶

Deliver the Bag of Lute Strings to receive the Cloak of the Jharin Apprentice and the Lute of the Jharin Apprentice.

Journeyman Cloak — NPC: Aljuum Mohim

▶
Items to Farm
  • 2x Young Sonic Wolf Jawbone
  • Aljuum's Favorite Drink (item 22154)
  • Wolf Bane Lore Tomes (buy from Arms Historian Qua)
Give Lute of the Jharin Apprentice to Aljuum Mohim → Blue Cloth Bag
▶

Hand Aljuum Mohim the Lute of the Jharin Apprentice. The lute will be returned to you along with a Blue Cloth Bag.

Buy Aljuum's favorite drink (item 22154) and turn it in
▶

Purchase item 22154 (Aljuum's favorite drink) and hand it over to him to advance the quest.

Say "wolves" → collect 2 young sonic wolf jawbones from Hollowshade Moor
▶

Say "wolves" to Aljuum Mohim. He will instruct you to fill the Blue Cloth Bag with 2 young sonic wolf jawbones. Hunt young sonic wolves in Hollowshade Moor for the jawbones.

Turn in filled bag → Jharin Hilt Piece
▶

Hand the filled Blue Cloth Bag to Aljuum Mohim to receive the Jharin Hilt Piece.

Show Jharin Hilt Piece to Hana Sulm → weapon sketches
▶

Present the Jharin Hilt Piece to Hana Sulm. She will provide you with the weapon sketches needed for crafting.

Buy Wolf Bane Lore tomes from Arms Historian Qua
▶

Purchase the Wolf Bane Lore tomes from Arms Historian Qua. These are required for crafting the Sonic Wolf Bane Dagger.

Craft the Sonic Wolf Bane Dagger
▶

Use the sketches and tomes to craft the Sonic Wolf Bane Dagger.

Turn in Dagger + Cloak of the Jharin Apprentice → Journeyman Cloak
▶

Hand in the Sonic Wolf Bane Dagger and your Cloak of the Jharin Apprentice. You will receive the Cloak of the Jharin Journeyman, and the dagger will be returned to you.

Dar Khura (Shaman)

Recruit Cloak — NPC: Spiritist Fehril

▶
Items to Farm
  • 3x Hairless Rockhopper Hide
  • Xakra Bile
  • Scorpion Blood
  • Water
Give Acrylia Slate to Spiritist Fehril
▶

Hand over your Acrylia Slate to Spiritist Fehril. He will task you with collecting 3 hairless rockhopper hides.

Turn in 3 hides → Hopperhide Buckler + brew Cask of Sweetwater
▶

Turn in the 3 hairless rockhopper hides to receive a Hopperhide Buckler and a brewing cask. Brew xakra bile + scorpion blood + water to make Sweetwater. Pour 2 flasks of Sweetwater into the cask to create the Cask of Sweetwater.

Turn in Cask of Sweetwater → Hopperhide Treatment; visit Merchant Ayyad → Ayyad's Seal
▶

Deliver the Cask of Sweetwater to receive the Hopperhide Treatment. Then visit Merchant Ayyad to obtain Ayyad's Seal.

Turn in Buckler + Treatment + Ayyad's Seal + Initiate's Cloak → Recruit Cloak
▶

Give Fehril the Hopperhide Buckler, Hopperhide Treatment, Ayyad's Seal, and your Initiate's Cloak. You will receive the Cloak of the Dar Khura Recruit, a Treated Buckler, and a Spell.

Apprentice Cloak — NPCs: Fharra Cawfeet, Mydi Darjik, Fehril

▶
Items to Farm
  • Dulfis Mushrooms
  • Wet Fang Eyes (bag full)
Show Treated Buckler to Fharra Cawfeet → tincture
▶

Present the Treated Buckler to Fharra Cawfeet to receive a tincture for the next step.

Take buckler to Mydi Darjik → craft Imbued Hopperhide Buckler
▶

Bring the buckler to Mydi Darjik to receive a Large Folded Cloth. Make a soaking solution from dulfis mushrooms, then wrap the buckler with the tincture using the cloth to create an Imbued Hopperhide Buckler.

Turn in Imbued Buckler to Mydi → bag for wet fang eyes
▶

Hand the Imbued Hopperhide Buckler to Mydi Darjik. She will give you a bag and task you with filling it with wet fang eyes.

Turn in Bag of Wet Fang Eyes → Mydi's Blessing token
▶

Deliver the filled Bag of Wet Fang Eyes to Mydi Darjik to receive Mydi's Blessing token.

Turn in token + Recruit Cloak + Imbued Buckler to Fehril → Apprentice Cloak
▶

Give Spiritist Fehril Mydi's Blessing token, your Recruit Cloak, and the Imbued Hopperhide Buckler. You will receive the Cloak of the Dar Khura Apprentice and an upgraded buckler.

Journeyman Cloak — NPCs: Jaima Seyel, Amat Iuziq

▶
Items to Farm
  • Sonic Wolf Paws (bag full)
  • Sonic Wolf Bane Books (buy from Arms Historian Qua)
Show Apprentice Cloak to Jaima Seyel → bag; fill with sonic wolf paws
▶

Show your Apprentice Cloak to Jaima Seyel to receive a bag. Hunt sonic wolves in Hollowshade Moor and fill the bag with sonic wolf paws.

Turn in bag → Note to Amat + Strainer
▶

Hand over the filled bag to receive a Note to Amat and a Strainer.

Take Note to Amat Iuziq → Voucher → Arrin Murij → Dar Khura Spear Shaft
▶

Deliver the Note to Amat Iuziq, who will give you a Voucher. Take the Voucher to Arrin Murij to receive the Dar Khura Spear Shaft.

Buy Sonic Wolf Bane books from Arms Historian Qua
▶

Purchase the necessary Sonic Wolf Bane tomes from Arms Historian Qua in Shar Vahl.

Craft the Sonic Wolf Bane Spear
▶

Use the spear shaft and the tomes to craft the Sonic Wolf Bane Spear.

Turn in Apprentice Cloak + Spear → Journeyman Cloak
▶

Give your Cloak of the Dar Khura Apprentice and the Sonic Wolf Bane Spear to the appropriate NPC. You will receive the Cloak of the Dar Khura Journeyman, and the spear will be returned.

Khala Dun (Warrior)

Recruit Cloak

▶
Items to Farm
  • 4x Grimling Femur
  • Acrylia Dust
Collect 4 grimling femurs → craft Crude Bone Short Sword
▶

Gather 4 grimling femur bones and use them to craft a Crude Bone Short Sword.

Complete acrylia dust errand → craft Buckler Frame (blacksmithing)
▶

Run the acrylia dust errand, then use blacksmithing to craft a Buckler Frame from the materials obtained.

Turn in Buckler Frame + Crude Bone Short Sword + Initiate's Cloak → Recruit Cloak
▶

Give the Buckler Frame, Crude Bone Short Sword, and Initiate's Cloak to your guildmaster. You will receive the Recruit Cloak, a Buckler, and a Skeletonbane Sword.

Apprentice Cloak

▶
Items to Farm
  • Acrylia Nuggets (from rhinobeetles)
  • Beetle Horns
Get acrylia nuggets from rhinobeetles → craft Leather Gripped Sword + bolts
▶

Farm acrylia nuggets from rhinobeetles. Use blacksmithing to craft a Leather Gripped Sword and a set of bolts.

Collect beetle horns → craft Horn Covered Buckler
▶

Gather beetle horns and combine them with your buckler to create the Horn Covered Buckler.

Turn in Horn Covered Buckler + Leather Gripped Sword + Recruit Cloak → Apprentice Cloak
▶

Hand over the Horn Covered Buckler, Leather Gripped Sword, and your Recruit Cloak to receive the Cloak of the Khala Dun Apprentice.

Journeyman Cloak

▶
Items to Farm
  • Sonic Wolf Paws
  • Blade / Claw / Hilt Molds
  • Wolf Bane Lore Tomes (buy from Arms Historian Qua)
Collect wolf paws in Hollowshade Moor → status report to Bookkeeper Leaha
▶

Hunt sonic wolves in Hollowshade Moor for their paws, then deliver a status report to Bookkeeper Leaha.

Obtain Blade/Claw/Hilt molds → craft Sonic Wolf Bane Dagger
▶

Acquire the Blade, Claw, and Hilt molds needed for weapon crafting. Purchase Wolf Bane Lore tomes from Arms Historian Qua, then craft the Sonic Wolf Bane Dagger.

Turn in Apprentice Cloak + Dagger → Journeyman Cloak
▶

Hand in your Cloak of the Khala Dun Apprentice and the Sonic Wolf Bane Dagger to receive the Cloak of the Khala Dun Journeyman.

Taruun (Rogue)

Recruit Cloak

▶
Items to Farm
  • 3x Soft Rhinobeetle Chitin
  • 4x Rockhopper Egg
  • Acrylia
Collect 3 soft rhinobeetle chitin → buckler + cooking recipe
▶

Gather 3 soft rhinobeetle chitin. Turn them in to receive a buckler and a cooking recipe.

Cook 4 boiled rockhopper eggs → deliver to Sergeant Tylah
▶

Use the cooking recipe to make 4 boiled rockhopper eggs, then deliver them to Sergeant Tylah.

Obtain acrylia → gather Harbin's and Rytan's Seals
▶

Acquire the needed acrylia material, then obtain both Harbin's Seal and Rytan's Seal to complete the authorization chain.

Turn in seals + Initiate's Cloak → Recruit Cloak
▶

Hand in Harbin's Seal, Rytan's Seal, and your Initiate's Cloak to receive the Cloak of the Taruun Recruit.

Apprentice Cloak

▶
Items to Farm
  • Spicy Fish Stew ingredients
Complete cave patrol → report to Elder Ternq
▶

Carry out the cave patrol task, then report your findings to Elder Ternq.

Make Spicy Fish Stew → deliver to Kerr Tiparr
▶

Cook the Spicy Fish Stew using the required ingredients, then deliver it to Kerr Tiparr.

Turn in Recruit Cloak + empty pot → Apprentice Cloak
▶

Hand in your Recruit Cloak and the empty cooking pot to receive the Cloak of the Taruun Apprentice.

Journeyman Cloak

▶
Items to Farm
  • Sonic Wolf Tongues
  • Sonic Wolf Bane Tomes (buy from Arms Historian Qua)
Collect sonic wolf tongues → Taruun Hilt Piece + Mortar and Pestle
▶

Hunt sonic wolves for their tongues, then turn them in to receive the Taruun Hilt Piece and a Mortar and Pestle.

Use Mortar and Pestle for poison making → buy tomes from Arms Historian Qua
▶

Use the Mortar and Pestle in the poison-making step of the crafting process. Purchase the Sonic Wolf Bane tomes from Arms Historian Qua.

Craft Sonic Wolf Bane Dagger → Turn in Apprentice Cloak + Dagger → Journeyman Cloak
▶

Craft the Sonic Wolf Bane Dagger using the hilt piece and tomes. Then hand in your Cloak of the Taruun Apprentice and the dagger to receive the Cloak of the Taruun Journeyman.

▶
Items to Farm

▶

Craft claws with forged fasteners

▶

Complete Rittuh Purrik task in Hollowshade Moor → collect glowing light source from caves
▶

Work with Rittuh Purrik on a task in Hollowshade Moor, then enter the caves to collect a glowing light source.

Wrap in Recruit Cloak → Apprentice Cloak
▶

Combine the glowing light source and other materials with your Recruit Cloak to receive the Cloak of the Khati Sha Apprentice.

Journeyman Cloak

▶
Items to Farm
  • Sonic Wolf Pup Molars
  • Stretching Dowels (from Jhimis Khadwi)
  • Sonic Wolf Bane Tomes (buy from Arms Historian Qua)
Collect sonic wolf pup molars → craft Claw Mounts
▶

Hunt sonic wolf pups for their molars, then use them to craft Claw Mounts.

Obtain stretching dowels from Jhimis Khadwi → buy tomes from Arms Historian Qua
▶

Get the stretching dowels from Jhimis Khadwi, then purchase the Sonic Wolf Bane tomes from Arms Historian Qua.

Craft Sonic Wolf Bane Claws → Turn in Apprentice Cloak + Claws → Journeyman Cloak
▶

Combine all materials to craft the Sonic Wolf Bane Claws. Then hand in your Cloak of the Khati Sha Apprentice and the Sonic Wolf Bane Claws to receive the Cloak of the Khati Sha Journeyman.

Garrison Cloak (All Classes)

Once you have your Journeyman's Cloak, all five classes follow the same final step to earn their Garrison Cloak. Speak with General Avilah to begin.

Turn in item 8467 to General Avilah → receive a box
▶

Give item 8467 to General Avilah to receive a box that must be filled with specific trophies from Hollowshade Moor named mobs.

Kill Hollowshade named mobs for three trophies
▶

Hunt the three named mobs in Hollowshade Moor to collect:

  • Grimling Chieftain Staff — from the Grimling Chieftain
  • Owlbear Guardian Talon — from the Owlbear Guardian
  • Sonic Wolf Guardian Fang — from the Sonic Wolf Guardian
Fill box with all three trophies + your Journeyman's Cloak → Garrison Cloak
▶

Place the Grimling Chieftain Staff, Owlbear Guardian Talon, Sonic Wolf Guardian Fang, and your Journeyman's Cloak into the box. Turn it in to receive your class-specific Garrison Cloak and a Garrison Enlistment Form.
