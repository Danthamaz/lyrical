Overview

The Shar Vahl cloak quest is a multi-tier progression for Vah Shir characters. Each of the five classes — Jharin (Bard), Dar Khura (Shaman), Khala Dun (Warrior), Taruun (Rogue), and Khati Sha (Beastlord) — has its own quest line that progresses through:

Initiate
→
Recruit
→
Apprentice
→
Journeyman
→
Garrison
→
Officer
→
Hero

All five Journeyman lines converge on crafting a Sonic Wolf Bane weapon using tomes from Arms Historian Qua.

Cross-class dependency: The Journeyman quest step requires items crafted by other Vah Shir classes. To complete it you will need to either:

  • Find other players of those classes who have completed the quest line up to that point and ask them to farm and craft the items for you
  • Complete all the class cloaks up to Journeyman yourself. You can do this on one character

Warning: In order to get the final Hero Cloak, you must complete the Grimling Forest war event. This is very difficult as it requires a sizeable raid force, and most players aren't interested in the content since there is no other loot. Only one person can receive the cloak reward per war.

Initiate — Citizenship (All Classes)

All five classes must complete the citizenship quest before beginning their class-specific cloak progression. This quest grants you your Initiate's Cloak of Shar Vahl and your Acrylia Slate of Shar Vahl.

Give Guild Summons to your class guildmaster
▶

Speak with your class guildmaster in Shar Vahl. Give them your Guild Summons to receive an Application for Citizenship.

Master Taruun Rakutah says, 'Good ______, I am pleased to see you. You have come of age and it is time for you to register for citizenship. Your invitation indicates that the Taruun, hunters and providers of Shar Vahl, have noticed you and consider your potential to be worthy of our training. First, take this application to Registrar Bindarah and return to me with proof of citizenship.'

Deliver Application for Citizenship to Registrar Bindarah
▶

Find Registrar Bindarah near the south gate and bridge at -390, +140. Give her the Certificate of Taxability.

Registrar Bindarah says, 'Young ______, I will be happy to process your registration for you. While I etch your name on our people's book of records I will require you to run a couple of errands. Take this certificate to the tax collector and obtain his seal. While you're out doing that, have Mignah create your personal Acrylia slate for you. Bring both the seal and the slate to me as soon as you can.'

You receive a Certificate of Taxability.

Get Certificate stamped by Tax Collector Khugra
▶

Find Tax Collector Khugra in the bank at -288, -453, -187. Give him the Certificate of Taxability.

Tax Collector Khugra places his seal on the certificate before returning it to you. 'Ahh, a new taxpayer, wonderful! You must always remember that it is a distinct privilege to contribute to the upkeep of our noble society and not merely a duty or a burden. I look forward to collecting your honorable taxes in the future. May the spirits prosper you, ______.'

You receive a Stamped Certificate of Taxability.

Obtain an Acrylia Slate from Mignah Cahru
▶

Find Mignah Cahru in the tavern near the north gate at +485, -285. Say "What about an acrylia slate?"

Mignah Cahru pulls a smoldering piece of forged acrylia from a nearby oven and, before you can react, presses your hand into the still soft metal. 'This may hurt a bit, but it'll heal.' He then etches your name and the date into the slate and plunges it into a bucket of water before placing it into your stinging palm.

You receive an Acrylia Slate.

Return Stamped Certificate + Acrylia Slate to Registrar Bindarah
▶

Give both the Stamped Certificate of Taxability and the Acrylia Slate to Registrar Bindarah. She keeps the slate for now — you'll get it back later.

Registrar Bindarah says, 'Ahh, there you are. I was about to send someone looking for you. Everything seems to be in order here, only one task remains. You must gain audience with the king and swear fealty to his highness by handing him this document. Return to me when this is done.'

You receive a Note to King Raja.

Give Note to King Raja Kerrath
▶

Take the note to King Raja Kerrath in the throne room of the palace at 0, +565, -160.

King Raja Kerrath says, 'Your humility and willingness to serve shall not be wasted. There is much to be done, ______, and our people thank you in advance for your selfless service.'

You receive a Note from King Raja.

Return Note from King Raja to Registrar Bindarah
▶

Bring the Note from King Raja back to Registrar Bindarah.

Registrar Bindarah says, 'Well done, ______, I am honored to be the first to welcome you to citizenship of Shar Vahl! May you serve our society as well as it serves you. Return to your guildmaster and present both the slate and the application to him. The acrylia slate shall henceforth serve as proof of your citizenship.'

Registrar Bindarah says, 'Oh, by the way, be careful with this as it will be important for recording your service to our society. If you should somehow lose it, ask me about your slate and I will issue you a new one.'

Your faction standing with Citizens of Shar Vahl got better (+400).

You receive an Acrylia Slate of Shar Vahl and a Notarized Application.

Give Notarized Application to your Guildmaster → Initiate's Cloak
▶

Return to your class guildmaster and give them the Notarized Application. You will receive your Initiate's Cloak of Shar Vahl.

Important: The Acrylia Slate is your unique identification for all subsequent Vah Shir quests. If lost, Registrar Bindarah can replace it.

Jharin (Bard)

Recruit Cloak — NPC: Gherik Alarhun

▶

Rewards: Cloak of the Jharin Recruit, Buckler of the Jharin Recruit, Drum of the Jharin Recruit (not used for anything — safe to delete).

Give Acrylia Slate to Gherik Alarhun
▶

Gherik Alarhun is at +257, +511 in the pottery store — facing the Arena, it's the last store on the left beside the Arena. He won't respond to hails; just give him your slate.

Gherik Alarhun says, 'Good day, ______, I've been expecting you. It is both my duty and my pleasure to oversee this early stage of your training. To begin, you must display basic combat capability. To this end, return to me with three shells of scorpions and I will instruct your further.'

Collect 3 scorpion shells and turn them in
▶

Kill scorpions in the Shar Vahl pit. The shells are uncommon drops. Turn in all 3 to Gherik.

Gherik Alarhun examines the shells, 'These will do fine. I will fashion a buckler from these shells that will help to preserve your life in combat. As I busy myself with its construction I send you on your next task. As a disciple of the Jharin you will become skilled in the art of pottery.'

Gherik Alarhun says, 'Grind the femur bone of the four grimlings in this container. Take the resulting powder and, in the same container, combine water and xakra worm bile. The resulting clay will yield a special bowl when spun with this sketch and a flask of water on a pottery wheel. Bring the unfired bowl to me.'

You receive a Claymixing Bowl and a reusable Bowl Sketch.

Craft the Unfired Jharin Bowl (Pottery)
▶

Kill skeleton grimlings in the Shar Vahl pit for 4 grimling femur bones (NO DROP, not stackable, uncommon). Also kill xakra worm larvae in the pit for xakra worm bile (NO DROP, stackable, common).

  1. Grind the 4 femur bones in the Claymixing Bowl to make bone powder
  2. In the same bowl, combine the powder with water and xakra worm bile to make clay
  3. On a pottery wheel, combine the clay + Bowl Sketch + Flask of Water to create an Unfired Jharin Bowl

The pottery combine can be tricky — one report succeeded at Pottery skill 36.

Turn in bowl → fire it → gather drum materials from Broker Fahaar
▶

Give the Unfired Bowl to Gherik.

Gherik Alarhun carefully inspects the bowl, 'Not bad for a beginner. Fire it in a kiln with a flask of water and a quality firing sheet. Broker Fahaar is in need of your help. It so happens that he can help you as well. He can supply you with the materials to transform your bowl into a drum that will enhance your percussion songs. Show him your acrylia slate and return to me with the bowl of the Jharin, your initiate's cloak, a stretched hopperhide and a leather cord.'

Fire the bowl in a kiln with a Flask of Water and a Quality Firing Sheet to create the Bowl of the Jharin.

Broker Fahaar is at +228, +24 — just before you enter the palace walls, to the right, under a merchant tent. Show him your Acrylia Slate.

Broker Fahaar says, 'Excellent, I can always use someone eager to work. I require young rockhopper hides to stretch for some special tailoring projects. Combine in this sack the hides of four young rockhoppers and return to me the full hopperhide sack, I will give you a stretched hide in return.'

He gives you a 4-slot Young Hopperhide Sack. Kill young rockhoppers in the Shar Vahl pit for hides (NO TRADE, not stackable, uncommon). Combine 4 hides in the sack and give the combined sack back to Fahaar. He gives you a Stretched Hopperhide and a Leather Cord.

Turn in Bowl + Initiate's Cloak + Hopperhide + Cord → Recruit Cloak
▶

Give Gherik the Bowl of the Jharin, your Initiate's Cloak, the Stretched Hopperhide, and the Leather Cord.

Gherik Alarhun binds the stretched hide to the bowl with the leather cord, 'Wonderful! You have done well and are ready for the next portion of your training. I have finished your buckler and enhanced your cloak. They will both prove to be very valuable to you in your service to the Vah Shir. Show your buckler to Hymnist Gawfed for further instruction. May your deeds bring honor to the Jharin!'

Gherik Alarhun shouts, 'People of Shar Vahl, it is with great pleasure that I announce the Jharin has accepted as an official recruit. This honorable citizen will henceforth be charged with the duty of supporting and magnifying the spirit of our people through song while always being eager to help any Vah Shir in need. Please help me in celebrating ______ coming of age!'

You receive: Cloak of the Jharin Recruit, Buckler of the Jharin Recruit, and a Drum.

Apprentice Cloak — NPC: Hymnist Gawfed

▶

Rewards: Cloak of the Jharin Apprentice, Lute of the Jharin Apprentice.

Show Buckler of the Jharin Recruit to Hymnist Gawfed
▶

Hymnist Gawfed is at -120, +90. Looking up the palace steps, he's in the building to the left. Hand him the Buckler of the Jharin Recruit.

Hymnist Gawfed says, 'Very well met, young friend. I assume that Gherik has sent you to me for the purpose of training you. Unfortunately, I will not be doing any formal training for the several days. I'm taking a bit of a holiday to celebrate my wife's birthday. If you could take this receipt to the merchant for me before my wife gets home, I'd appreciate it. I'll place you at the top of my training list as well.'

Say "What merchant?"

Hymnist Gawfed says, 'Gah... I'm losing my wits. I didn't tell you which merchant. Well, the fur is the first thing to go, and the mind is a close second. My wife's birthday is usually quite a large event in my household and I've been trying to make this one extra special. I'm lucky I can still find my tail. Take the note to Jihli Mahej. He's an importer who lives near the... gah! I can't remember the address. I'm sure you'll find him though. Good luck!'

You receive Gawfed's Receipt.

Complete the vase errand: Jihli Mahej → Chogar → pottery
▶

Jihli Mahej is in the building to the left of the Registrar. The vase Gawfed ordered was broken, so Jihli directs you to Chogar to repair it.

Chogar is under a tent in the southern-most section of town at -483, -313. He gives you a Rough Vase Sketch (returned on failure).

  1. On a pottery wheel, combine a Block of Clay + Water + Rough Vase Sketch to create an Unfired Vase
  2. Fire the vase in a kiln with a Quality Firing Sheet

Trivial for this combine is between 85 and 95 Pottery skill.

Give vase to Gawfed → collect owlbear feathers in Hollowshade Moor
▶

Take the finished vase back to Hymnist Gawfed.

Hymnist Gawfed looks at the vase and nods his head in approval. 'I know that she's going to love this vase. The craftsmanship is amazing!' He says as he admires the vase. 'I need to properly thank you for retrieving it for me and I think that I know just the thing. If you gather some owlbear feathers I can have my brother apply them to your cloak. He can make a cloak really stand out. It will be a prize fit for any aspiring apprentice to wear. Take this sack and fill it with seven feathers and your recruit's cloak. Then return it to me with your cloak. I'll have it all sent over to my brother together in one bag.'

You receive a Large Cloth Sack (make sure you have a container slot open).

Owlbear feathers drop from owlbear cubs, fleshrenders, and razorbeaks in Hollowshade Moor, as well as grimy owlbear cubs in Paludal Caverns.

Combine 7 feathers + Recruit Cloak in sack → give Bag of Feathers to Gawfed
▶

Place 7 owlbear feathers and your Cloak of the Jharin Recruit into the Large Cloth Sack to create a Bag of Feathers. Give it to Hymnist Gawfed.

Hymnist Gawfed says, 'It looks like you're all set. I'll have this sent over to my brother at once. Thank you again for fetching the vase for me. I can't wait to give it to the missus. I just know that she's going to love it. I have another task for you. It shouldn't take much time at all. Your cloak won't be done for a while, so you can do this while you wait. Take this piece of parchment to Jangle. He'll give you a bag of lute strings. I'm restringing all of my instruments before the party. Please be quick. Thank you again.'

You receive a Note for Jangle, plus experience and positive Jharin faction.

Lute string chain: Jangle → owlbear guts → Jangle → Gawfed → Jangle
▶

Jangle is at +238, -494. Give him the Note for Jangle.

Jangle says, 'I would love to fill this order for you friend, but the lad that we sent out into the moor to hunt up our last batch of owlbear guts has not returned. No guts, no strings. I'm afraid that I have neither. If your master will allow it, perhaps you could go gather the guts for me. If you complete this task, I will waive the fee for his next couple of orders. I'm sure that he'll find that proposal pleasant enough. Here's a bag to gather the guts in. Please fill it before you return to me.'

You receive Jangle's Bag. Go to Hollowshade Moor and collect 8 owlbear guts from owlbear cubs. They look like Fatty Owlbear Meat — look before you loot, as the bag won't combine if you mix items.

Combine 8 guts in the bag to craft a Bag of Owlbear Guts. Give it to Jangle.

Jangle says, 'Thank you for gathering these materials for me. This bag should last for some time. I'll need to clean this bag and make the strings. While I'm doing that, please take this note back to Gawfed and have him specify how many of each string he wants. He only listed the sizes in his note, not the quantities.'

You receive a Note from Jangle plus experience and faction. Give the note to Hymnist Gawfed.

Hymnist Gawfed says, 'I really must be losing my mind. I could have sworn I specified the quantities. Oh well, I apologize for the confusion. Please take this back to him with my apologies.'

You receive a Note to Jangle. Hand it to Jangle.

Jangle says, 'Very good, he's got it all written down this time. These strings should work nicely for him, and let's not mention the fact that they are absolutely free. Take care and remember to shop here for all of your Jharin needs.'

You receive a Bag of Lute Strings plus experience and faction.

Turn in Bag of Lute Strings to Gawfed → Apprentice Cloak + Lute
▶

Give the Bag of Lute Strings to Hymnist Gawfed.

Hymnist Gawfed says, 'My dear friend, ______, you have been a wondrous help! Though we have not had an opportunity to work on the more refined Jharin teachings, you have definitely proven yourself to be an honorable citizen and worthy apprentice. My brother has returned with your cloak. Please take it along with the title of Jharin Apprentice. To further demonstrate my appreciation of your work for me, please take this lute.'

Hymnist Gawfed shouts, 'Everyone, please welcome my student and friend, ______, to the rank of Jharin Apprentice!'

Hymnist Gawfed says, 'Now, please meet with Aljuum Mohim. He will be responsible for your training during my holiday. Please show him the lute that I just gave you. He'll most likely be in a tavern somewhere. I just hope that he is sober. Take care, friend!'

You receive: Cloak of the Jharin Apprentice (AC 6, STA +1, CHA +2, HP +5, Pottery +2%) and Lute of the Jharin Apprentice (CHA +2).

Journeyman Cloak — NPC: Aljuum Mohim

▶

This step requires cooperation from other Vah Shir classes. Crafting the Sonic Wolf Bane weapon needs components from all five classes: Bard, Beastlord, Rogue, Shaman, and Warrior.

Give Lute of the Jharin Apprentice to Aljuum Mohim
▶

Aljuum Mohim can be found in a bar in the south end of town (the "slums"). Hail him and give him the Lute of the Jharin Apprentice.

Aljuum Mohim says, 'That is quite a lute. Gawfed has an incredible gift in that area. I don't have the patience to build instruments. I prefer to use my time for other things. I'm sure that Gawfed will fill you in on the more delicate side of our craft when he gets over his holiday. Until that time, I'll get you started on the other part of being a Jharin. Here, take your lute and this bag. Once you get that stowed away, buy me a drink. If you can guess my favorite drink, I'll start you on your lesson.'

You receive a Blue Cloth Bag. The lute is returned to you.

Buy Aljuum a Sonic Wolf Pale Ale
▶

His favorite drink is Sonic Wolf Pale Ale, available from the nearby Barkeep Rawlf for about 8 sp. Give it to Aljuum.

Aljuum Mohim sips the drink and smiles. 'Very good! I guess I should get you started.' He says as he finishes the drink. 'Our kin have encountered a number of beasts since the time of our arrival here. Your lesson today will center around one such creature. Maybe that will give you a better understanding of our role here. Bah, Taruun scouts... a real Jharin can do twice as much in combat... well, I digress. You're here to learn about wolves, not my petty animosities.'

Say "What wolves?"

Aljuum Mohim says, 'The wolves that I speak of have no need for sight. They are products of the darkness that surrounds us. Their ability to move in the darkness and attack in packs makes them a serious threat to any Vah Shir that walks beyond our city's gates. I fear them more than I fear the twisted little people that live in the caves around here. It is the duty of each citizen to help control the number of these creatures. Fill the bag that I just handed you with the jawbones of two young sonic wolves and I'll know that you have a real understanding of the ferocity of those creatures. If you return, I'll have something that you may find interesting.'

Collect 2 Young Sonic Wolf Jawbones from Hollowshade Moor
▶

Young Sonic Wolf Jawbones drop from Sonic Wolf Pups and Sonic Wolflings in Hollowshade Moor. Combine 2 jawbones in the Blue Cloth Bag to create a Bloody Cloth Bag. Give it to Aljuum.

Aljuum Mohim says, 'How was your little encounter with those beasts? Quite nasty in combat, eh? I told you as much. Well, now that you've seen them firsthand, I'll tell you how to deal with them like a true hunter. Take this hilt piece and show Hana Sulm. She'll give you some sketches. Once you have those sketches, go to Arms Historian Qua and purchase the tomes on Wolf Bane Lore or something like that. He'll know what you mean. You can use those books and the sketches to make a weapon that offers considerable power against those wolves. Once you have the weapon, bring it to me with your apprentice cloak. I'll have something for you. Now go away and let me finish this drink.'

You receive a Jharin Hilt Piece.

Show Jharin Hilt Piece to Hana Sulm → weapon sketches
▶

Find Hana Sulm at approximately -463, -319. Give her the hilt piece.

Hana Sulm says, 'Well, you're going to need to practice for a while to make these things. Take these sketches and read up on the lore concerning sonic wolves. You'll learn how to use the sketches. Practice all you can. I'm sure that you're going to have a lot of people coming to you with requests.'

You get the hilt piece back plus two NO DROP sketches: Blade Lining Sketches and Claw Lining Sketches.

Buy Wolf Bane Lore tomes from Arms Historian Qua
▶

Purchase the Wolf Bane Lore tomes from Arms Historian Qua in Shar Vahl. Also buy a Weapon Assembly Kit from the same vendor.

Craft the Sonic Wolf Bane Dagger (cross-class)
▶

This weapon requires components crafted by all five Vah Shir classes. The bard's contribution is a Ceramic Dagger Sleeve (or Ceramic Claw Sleeve Set for beastlords).

Bard's crafting step — Ceramic Dagger Sleeve:

  1. On a pottery wheel, combine: Ashes of Dark Rituals, Black Silt Clay, Blade Lining Sketches, Owlbear Bile, Sonic Wolf Blood, and a Water Flask → Unfinished Ceramic Dagger Sleeve (trivial ~135)
  2. Fire in kiln with a Quality Firing Sheet → Ceramic Dagger Sleeve

(For beastlord claws, use Claw Lining Sketches instead of Blade Lining Sketches.)

Final assembly — Weapon Assembly Kit:

Combine in the Weapon Assembly Kit (no-fail combine):

  • Ceramic Dagger Sleeve (bard-crafted)
  • Sonic Wolf Bane — poison crafted by rogues
  • Toughened Sinew Lashings — crafted by beastlords
  • Porous Dagger Blade — crafted by warriors
  • Reactive Sealant Soak — crafted by shamen

Result: Sonic Wolf Bane Dagger

Turn in Dagger + Apprentice Cloak to Aljuum → Journeyman Cloak
▶

Give the Sonic Wolf Bane Dagger and your Cloak of the Jharin Apprentice to Aljuum Mohim. You will receive the Cloak of the Jharin Journeyman and the dagger will be returned to you.

▶

▶

Take buckler to Mydi Darjik → craft Imbued Hopperhide Buckler
▶

Bring the buckler to Mydi Darjik to receive a Large Folded Cloth. Make a soaking solution from dulfis mushrooms, then wrap the buckler with the tincture using the cloth to create an Imbued Hopperhide Buckler.

Turn in Imbued Buckler to Mydi → bag for wet fang eyes
▶

Hand the Imbued Hopperhide Buckler to Mydi Darjik. She will give you a bag and task you with filling it with wet fang eyes.

Turn in Bag of Wet Fang Eyes → Mydi's Blessing token
▶

Deliver the filled Bag of Wet Fang Eyes to Mydi Darjik to receive Mydi's Blessing token.

Turn in token + Recruit Cloak + Imbued Buckler to Fehril → Apprentice Cloak
▶

Give Spiritist Fehril Mydi's Blessing token, your Recruit Cloak, and the Imbued Hopperhide Buckler. You will receive the Cloak of the Dar Khura Apprentice and an upgraded buckler.

Journeyman Cloak — NPCs: Jaima Seyel, Amat Iuziq

▶
Show Apprentice Cloak to Jaima Seyel → bag; fill with sonic wolf paws
▶

Show your Apprentice Cloak to Jaima Seyel to receive a bag. Hunt sonic wolves in Hollowshade Moor and fill the bag with sonic wolf paws.

Turn in bag → Note to Amat + Strainer

Take Note to Amat Iuziq → Voucher → Arrin Murij → Dar Khura Spear Shaft

Buy Sonic Wolf Bane books from Arms Historian Qua

Purchase the necessary Sonic Wolf Bane tomes from Arms Historian Qua in Shar Vahl.

Craft the Sonic Wolf Bane Spear
▶

Use the spear shaft and the tomes to craft the Sonic Wolf Bane Spear.

Turn in Apprentice Cloak + Spear → Journeyman Cloak
▶

Give your Cloak of the Dar Khura Apprentice and the Sonic Wolf Bane Spear to the appropriate NPC. You will receive the Cloak of the Dar Khura Journeyman, and the spear will be returned.

Garrison Cloak (All Classes)

Once you have your Journeyman's Cloak, all five classes follow the same path. Ahom Guzhin is just inside the tunnel from Hollowshade Moor to Grimling Forest, off to the left behind a rock.

Give your Journeyman's Cloak to Ahom Guzhin
▶

Ahom Guzhin says, 'Please do not think poorly of me, but I must see your Journeyman's cloak if you wish to converse. Be quick about it. This place is not completely safe.'

Give him your class-specific Journeyman Cloak.

Ahom Guzhin says, 'Ah, there it is... I haven't seen one of those in a while. I remember when I first became a journeyman myself. That was many seasons and twice as many trails ago. Time becomes most deft in the period just after you figure that you've got a grasp on it.'

Ahom Guzhin says, 'Well, let us get back to pebbles and tax, shall we? You displayed a great deal of bravery in coming out here and looking for me. I fear my age is beginning to show. I can't evade danger as I did in my youth. With that in mind, I would like you to take this journal back to town for me. Historian Sharar will tend to my notes for me. She is a trusted friend and a valuable scholar. Please give it to her with my thanks.'

He gives you Ahom's Journal.

Give journal to Historian Sharar → Earring of the Owlbear + Note
▶

Go to Shar Vahl and find Historian Sharar in the Palace courtyard, in the library area to the right as you enter from the merchants area. Give her the journal.

Historian Sharar says, 'I see that you've found the codger. He really should settle down and come in from the weather. I often find myself worrying about him these days. Adventure takes its toll on everyone. I'd like to see him settle down before the moor takes a final toll from him.'

Historian Sharar says, 'Well, I can't say that it isn't a pleasure to have his journal. He's documented the moor with such splendid detail. I cannot wait to see what he's written in these last few seasons. It is all so very helpful. Just take a look at these earplugs. They are a product of his last set of notes. He came up with the idea, while hunting owlbear in the moor. They are supposed to keep the sound they make from harming you. You can keep them, if you take this note to General Avilah for me.'

You receive an Earring of the Owlbear (NO DROP, unlimited charges of Screech Immunity, usable at level 20+) and a Note for General Avilah.

Deliver Note to General Avilah → receive box for war trophies
▶

Find General Avilah in the north section of Shar Vahl, in a room on the third floor of a building reachable via outside stairs. Give her the note.

General Avilah says, 'Thank you for delivering this. While I have you here, perhaps you can help. We are in dire need of assistance in the moor. All of the reports from our Taruun are indicating the same thing. The area has to be swept clear of the factions that currently inhabit it. The land itself seems defendable, we need someone to gather some soldiers and clear the area.'

General Avilah says, 'If you accomplish this mission for us, I will be able to reward you for your actions in this leadership role. You will need only to return this box to me to collect your reward and promotion. It will need to contain a grimling chieftain staff, an owlbear guardian talon, a sonic wolf guardian fang, and your Journeyman's Cloak. Good luck to you, should you elect to accept this challenge.'

She gives you a Long Wooden Box (4-slot, GIANT capacity).

Kill Hollowshade named mobs for three trophies
▶

Hunt the three named mobs in Hollowshade Moor for rare drops:

  • Grimling Chieftain Staff — from grimling chieftains
  • Owlbear Guardian Talon — from owlbear guardians
  • Sonic Wolf Guardian Fang — from sonic wolf guardians
Combine trophies + Journeyman's Cloak in box → give to General Avilah → Garrison Cloak
▶

Place the Grimling Chieftain Staff, Owlbear Guardian Talon, Sonic Wolf Guardian Fang, and your Journeyman's Cloak into the Long Wooden Box. Combine to create a Box of War Trophies. Give it to General Avilah.

General Avilah says, 'You have proven to be a great leader and excellent soldier. Please accept this cloak as a symbol of your accomplishment. We would be proud to have you as a member of our ranks. You can choose to stay within the ranks of your sect or you can work with us as a member of the Shar Vahl Garrison. If you wish to continue your work as a member of the garrison, please take this form, sign it in triplicate and give it to Captain Darznel. Either way, please stand still, while I offer you our formal salutation.'

General Avilah shouts, 'Ahem... Citizens of Shar Vahl, Attention! It is my duty and honor as commander of the Shar Vahl Garrison to thank ______ for their fine work. Give 'em your complete respect and salute 'em when you see them in passing. We enjoy our freedom, because of brave citizens like ______. Thank you for your time... Carry on!'

You receive your class-specific Garrison Cloak and a Garrison Enlistment Form to take to Captain Darznel.

Officer's Cloak (All Classes)

Give Garrison Enlistment Form to Captain Darznel
▶

Hand Captain Darznel the form given to you by General Avilah after completing the Garrison Cloak quest.

Captain Darznel says, 'Let's see, yes... yes... It looks like you signed everything in the correct spot. That can only mean that you're not only brave, but you have some wits as well. If you have all of your teeth and can stand on one leg until the count of five, I think we can make use of you.'

Captain Darznel says, 'Take this Polished Acrylia Sphere and give it to Scout Husman. He's been leading raiding parties against the grunt camps. Adventures are great, but we need a real soldier to assist him this time. Perhaps we will get better results with your help.'

Captain Darznel says, 'After you complete that raid, please go with Scouts Danarin and Derrin. Once you secure those camps, bring me any grimling intelligence reports or documents that you may have found. Place the documents and your Garrison Cloak in this bag and bring them back to me. Complete this task and you may even get a promotion.'

Captain Darznel gives you a 4-slot box and a Polished Acrylia Sphere.

Complete three grimling camp raids with Scouts Husman, Danarin, and Derrin
▶

Give the Polished Acrylia Sphere to Scout Husman to begin the first raid. Then complete raids with Scouts Danarin and Derrin. Loot the Captured Grimling Documents I, II, and III dropped by each camp's officer.

Combine documents + Garrison Cloak in box → give to Captain Darznel → Officer's Cloak
▶

Place Captured Grimling Documents I, II, and III plus your Garrison Cloak into the 4-slot box and combine. Give the result to Captain Darznel.

Captain Darznel says, 'This documentation is a great start. Judging by the amount of material here, we'll be able to formulate a new battle within the week. It shouldn't take our linguists too long to decipher this scribble.'

Captain Darznel says, 'I cannot help but think that your presence and leadership are what made those missions such wonderful successes. I have been authorized to give you a field promotion for your valor and bravery in combat. Your ability to lead was the keystone in the success of those engagements. Welcome to the rank of Garrison Officer, ______.'

You receive your Officer's Cloak and a 2-slot container for the next series of raids.
