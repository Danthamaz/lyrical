Overview

The Shar Vahl cloak quest is a multi-tier progression for Vah Shir characters. Each of the five classes — Jharin (Bard), Dar Khura (Shaman), Khala Dun (Warrior), Taruun (Rogue), and Khati Sha (Beastlord) — has its own quest line that progresses through:

All five Journeyman lines converge on crafting a Sonic Wolf Bane weapon using tomes from Arms Historian Qua.

Cross-class dependency: The Journeyman quest step requires items crafted by other Vah Shir classes. To complete it you will need to either:

  • Find other players of those classes who have completed the quest line up to that point and ask them to farm and craft the items for you
  • Complete all the class cloaks up to Journeyman yourself. You can do this on one character

Warning: In order to get the final Hero Cloak, you must complete the Grimling Forest war event. This is very difficult as it requires a sizeable raid force, and most players aren't interested in the content since there is no other loot. Only one person can receive the cloak reward per war.

Initiate — Citizenship (All Classes)

Citizenship Quest — NPC: Registrar Bindarah

▶

Reward: Initiate's Cloak of Shar Vahl + Acrylia Slate of Shar Vahl

Tradeskills: None — NPC turn-ins only

All five classes must complete the citizenship quest before beginning their class-specific cloak progression.

Give Guild Summons to your class guildmaster
▶

Speak with your class guildmaster in Shar Vahl. Give them your Guild Summons to receive an Application for Citizenship.

Master Taruun Rakutah says, 'Good ______, I am pleased to see you. You have come of age and it is time for you to register for citizenship. Your invitation indicates that the Taruun, hunters and providers of Shar Vahl, have noticed you and consider your potential to be worthy of our training. First, take this application to Registrar Bindarah and return to me with proof of citizenship.'

Deliver Application for Citizenship to Registrar Bindarah
▶

Find Registrar Bindarah near the south gate and bridge at -390, +140. Give her the Application for Citizenship.

Registrar Bindarah says, 'Young ______, I will be happy to process your registration for you. While I etch your name on our people's book of records I will require you to run a couple of errands. Take this certificate to the tax collector and obtain his seal. While you're out doing that, have Mignah create your personal Acrylia slate for you. Bring both the seal and the slate to me as soon as you can.'

You receive a Certificate of Taxability.

Get Certificate stamped by Tax Collector Khugra
▶

Find Tax Collector Khugra in the bank at -288, -453, -187. Give him the Certificate of Taxability.

Tax Collector Khugra places his seal on the certificate before returning it to you. 'Ahh, a new taxpayer, wonderful! You must always remember that it is a distinct privilege to contribute to the upkeep of our noble society and not merely a duty or a burden. I look forward to collecting your honorable taxes in the future. May the spirits prosper you, ______.'

You receive a Stamped Certificate of Taxability.

Obtain an Acrylia Slate from Mignah Cahru
▶

Find Mignah Cahru in the tavern near the north gate at +485, -285. Say "acrylia slate".

Mignah Cahru pulls a smoldering piece of forged acrylia from a nearby oven and, before you can react, presses your hand into the still soft metal. 'This may hurt a bit, but it'll heal.' He then etches your name and the date into the slate and plunges it into a bucket of water before placing it into your stinging palm.

You receive an Acrylia Slate.

Return Stamped Certificate + Acrylia Slate to Registrar Bindarah
▶

Give both the Stamped Certificate of Taxability and the Acrylia Slate to Registrar Bindarah. She keeps the slate for now — you'll get it back later.

Registrar Bindarah says, 'Ahh, there you are. I was about to send someone looking for you. Everything seems to be in order here, only one task remains. You must gain audience with the king and swear fealty to his highness by handing him this document. Return to me when this is done.'

You receive a Note to King Raja.

Give Note to King Raja Kerrath
▶

Take the note to King Raja Kerrath in the throne room of the palace at 0, +565, -160.

King Raja Kerrath says, 'Your humility and willingness to serve shall not be wasted. There is much to be done, ______, and our people thank you in advance for your selfless service.'

You receive a Note from King Raja.

Return Note from King Raja to Registrar Bindarah
▶

Bring the Note from King Raja back to Registrar Bindarah.

Registrar Bindarah says, 'Well done, ______, I am honored to be the first to welcome you to citizenship of Shar Vahl! May you serve our society as well as it serves you. Return to your guildmaster and present both the slate and the application to him. The acrylia slate shall henceforth serve as proof of your citizenship.'

Registrar Bindarah says, 'Oh, by the way, be careful with this as it will be important for recording your service to our society. If you should somehow lose it, ask me about your slate and I will issue you a new one.'

Your faction standing with Citizens of Shar Vahl got better (+400).

You receive an Acrylia Slate of Shar Vahl and a Notarized Application.

Give Notarized Application to your Guildmaster → Initiate's Cloak
▶

Return to your class guildmaster and give them the Notarized Application and your Acrylia Slate of Shar Vahl (both items together).

Guildmasters: Elder Hymnist Hortitosh (Bard), Elder Spiritist Grawleh (Shaman), High Armsman Trukhanah (Warrior), Master Taruun Rakutah (Rogue), Elder Animist Sahdi (Beastlord).

You receive your Initiate's Cloak of Shar Vahl and the Acrylia Slate is returned. Each guildmaster also directs you to your first class trainer: Gherik Alarhun (Bard), Spiritist Fehril (Shaman), Khala Dun Hebijeb (Warrior), Harbin Gernawl (Rogue), Animist Poren (Beastlord).

Important: The Acrylia Slate is your unique identification for all subsequent Vah Shir quests. If lost, Registrar Bindarah can replace it.

Jharin (Bard)

Recruit Cloak — NPC: Gherik Alarhun

▶

Reward: Cloak of the Jharin Recruit, Buckler of the Jharin Recruit, Drum of the Jharin Recruit (not used for anything — safe to delete).

Tradeskills: Pottery (low trivial ~36)

Items to Farm
  • 3x Shell of a Scorpion (from scorpions in the Shar Vahl pit)
  • 4x Skeletal Grimling Femur (from grimling skeletons in the Shar Vahl pit, NO DROP)
  • 1x Xakra Bile (from xakra worm larvae in the Shar Vahl pit, NO DROP)
  • 4x Young Rockhopper Hide (from young rockhoppers in the Shar Vahl pit, NO DROP)
Give Acrylia Slate to Gherik Alarhun
▶

Gherik Alarhun is at +257, +511 in the pottery store — facing the Arena, it's the last store on the left beside the Arena. He won't respond to hails; just give him your slate.

Gherik Alarhun says, 'Good day, ______, I've been expecting you. It is both my duty and my pleasure to oversee this early stage of your training. To begin, you must display basic combat capability. To this end, return to me with three shells of scorpions and I will instruct you further.'

Collect 3 scorpion shells and turn them in
▶

Kill scorpions in the Shar Vahl pit. The shells are uncommon drops. Turn in all 3 to Gherik.

Gherik Alarhun examines the shells, 'These will do fine. I will fashion a buckler from these shells that will help to preserve your life in combat. As I busy myself with its construction I send you on your next task. As a disciple of the Jharin you will become skilled in the art of pottery.'

Gherik Alarhun says, 'Grind the femur bone of the four grimlings in this container. Take the resulting powder and, in the same container, combine water and xakra worm bile. The resulting clay will yield a special bowl when spun with this sketch and a flask of water on a pottery wheel. Bring the unfired bowl to me.'

You receive a Claymixing Bowl and a reusable Bowl Sketch.

Craft the Unfired Jharin Bowl (Pottery)
▶

Kill grimling skeletons in the Shar Vahl pit for 4 Skeletal Grimling Femurs (NO DROP, not stackable, uncommon). Also kill xakra worm larvae in the pit for Xakra Bile (NO DROP, stackable, common).

  1. Grind the 4 femur bones in the Claymixing Bowl to make bone powder
  2. In the same bowl, combine the powder with water and xakra worm bile to make clay
  3. On a pottery wheel, combine the clay + Bowl Sketch + Flask of Water to create an Unfired Jharin Bowl

The pottery combine can be tricky — one report succeeded at Pottery skill 36.

Turn in bowl → fire it → gather drum materials from Broker Fahaar
▶

Give the Unfired Bowl to Gherik.

Gherik Alarhun carefully inspects the bowl, 'Not bad for a beginner. Fire it in a kiln with a flask of water and a quality firing sheet. Broker Fahaar is in need of your help. It so happens that he can help you as well. He can supply you with the materials to transform your bowl into a drum that will enhance your percussion songs. Show him your acrylia slate and return to me with the bowl of the Jharin, your initiate's cloak, a stretched hopperhide and a leather cord.'

Fire the bowl in a kiln with a Flask of Water and a Quality Firing Sheet to create the Bowl of the Jharin.

Broker Fahaar is at +228, +24 — just before you enter the palace walls, to the right, under a merchant tent. Show him your Acrylia Slate.

Broker Fahaar says, 'Excellent, I can always use someone eager to work. I require young rockhopper hides to stretch for some special tailoring projects. Combine in this sack the hides of four young rockhoppers and return to me the full hopperhide sack, I will give you a stretched hide in return.'

He gives you a 4-slot Young Hopperhide Sack. Kill young rockhoppers in the Shar Vahl pit for Young Rockhopper Hides (NO DROP, not stackable, uncommon). Combine 4 hides in the sack and give the combined sack back to Fahaar.

Broker Fahaar says, 'Very nice. I thank you, ______. Here is the stretched hide I promised and a leather cord I made from the scraps. If you have no need for the hide itself, any merchant will purchase it from you. If you desire to become my apprentice, save four of the cords that you earn from me and return them when you desire to learn more. May the spirits guide and protect you!'

He gives you a Stretched Hopperhide and a Leather Cord.

Turn in Bowl + Initiate's Cloak + Hopperhide + Cord → Recruit Cloak
▶

Give Gherik the Bowl of the Jharin, your Initiate's Cloak, the Stretched Hopperhide, and the Leather Cord.

Gherik Alarhun binds the stretched hide to the bowl with the leather cord, 'Wonderful! You have done well and are ready for the next portion of your training. I have finished your buckler and enhanced your cloak. They will both prove to be very valuable to you in your service to the Vah Shir. Show your buckler to Hymnist Gawfed for further instruction. May your deeds bring honor to the Jharin!'

Gherik Alarhun shouts, 'People of Shar Vahl, it is with great pleasure that I announce the Jharin has accepted as an official recruit. This honorable citizen will henceforth be charged with the duty of supporting and magnifying the spirit of our people through song while always being eager to help any Vah Shir in need. Please help me in celebrating ______ coming of age!'

You receive: Cloak of the Jharin Recruit, Buckler of the Jharin Recruit, and a Drum.

Give vase to Gawfed → collect owlbear feathers in Hollowshade Moor

Aljuum Mohim says, 'The wolves that I speak of have no need for sight. They are products of the darkness that surrounds us. I fear them more than I fear the twisted little people that live in the caves around here. It is the duty of each citizen to help control the number of these creatures. Fill the bag that I just handed you with the jawbones of two young sonic wolves and I'll know that you have a real understanding of the ferocity of those creatures. If you return, I'll have something that you may find interesting.'

Well, now that you've seen them firsthand, I'll tell you how to deal with them like a true hunter. Take this hilt piece and show Hana Sulm. She'll give you some sketches. Once you have those sketches, go to Arms Historian Qua and purchase the tomes on Wolf Bane Lore or something like that. He'll know what you mean. You can use those books and the sketches to make a weapon that offers considerable power against those wolves. Once you have the weapon, bring it to me with your apprentice cloak. I'll have something for you. Now go away and let me finish this drink.'

You receive a Jharin Hilt Piece.

Important: The Jharin Hilt Piece is NO DROP — save it for Journeyman crafting.

Find Hana Sulm at approximately -463, -319. Give her the hilt piece.
