Overview

Vex Thal is the pinnacle raid zone of Luclin. Gaining access requires assembling The Scepter of Shadows (the Key to Vex Thal) through a multi-step process involving items from across Luclin's endgame zones.

The quest has three main components:

  1. Shadowed Scepter Frame — obtained from The Spirit of Akelha`Ra in Akheva Ruins
  2. 10 Lucid Shards — dropped by trash mobs across Luclin zones
  3. A Glowing Orb of Luclinite — dropped by raid bosses across Luclin

The assembly is a two-step tradeskill combine: first you combine the Shadowed Scepter Frame with the 10 Lucid Shards to create the Unadorned Scepter of Shadows, then you combine that with the Glowing Orb of Luclinite to create The Scepter of Shadows. The Scepter must be in your inventory to zone into Vex Thal through the entrance in Umbral Plains.

Prerequisites

  • Ability to survive in Akheva Ruins, The Deep, Maiden's Eye, Dawnshroud Peaks, Scarlet Desert, The Grey, Acrylia Caverns, Fungus Grove, and Ssraeshza Temple
  • A raid force for obtaining the Glowing Orb of Luclinite from any of the boss targets
  • A Summoned: Wisp Stone to turn in to A shimmering presence in Akheva Ruins to spawn the Spirit
  • An Essence Emerald (summoned item) to turn in to The Spirit of Akelha`Ra for the Scepter Frame

Step 1: Shadowed Scepter Frame

The Shadowed Scepter Frame is a 3-slot container obtained from The Spirit of Akelha`Ra in Akheva Ruins. This is the container you use for the first combine.

The Spirit of Akelha`Ra Quest

▶
Items Needed
  • Summoned: Wisp Stone (to spawn the Spirit)
  • Essence Emerald (to give the Spirit for the Frame)
Find A shimmering presence in Akheva Ruins
▶

A shimmering presence is a questable NPC found within Akheva Ruins. Hail the presence and go through the dialogue about memories, the great temple, and ornate robes to hear the story of the scepter being shattered by a storm.

Turn in a Summoned: Wisp Stone to spawn The Spirit of Akelha`Ra
▶

Give a Summoned: Wisp Stone to A shimmering presence. This causes the presence to transform, spawning The Spirit of Akelha`Ra (level 65).

Note: The Spirit of Akelha`Ra has a 30-minute despawn timer once spawned.

Speak with The Spirit of Akelha`Ra and turn in an Essence Emerald
▶

Hail the Spirit and follow the dialogue about her tormented prison, spirits, Atenha`Ra, and Vex Thal. She explains that her scepter was shattered in the storm and you need to reassemble it. She mentions you will need to "merge an Orb of Luclinite with a source of celestial power inside the scepter" and warns that "the emperor of the Shissar holds the power to manipulate such energy."

Give the Spirit an Essence Emerald. She absorbs the energy, chants in Akhevan, and disappears, leaving behind the Shadowed Scepter Frame for you.

Step 2: The 10 Lucid Shards

There are 10 unique Lucid Shards, each dropped by specific mobs in different Luclin zones. Each shard has an internal code that makes it unique. Every shard is No Drop, and you need exactly one of each.

Shards drop at varying rates from the listed mobs, with a shared lockout timer of approximately 30 days (2,629,800 seconds). This means each shard can only drop once per character per month from a given source.

The sun revenants are the better target with a higher drop chance. Sun revenants are undead and can see through invisibility.

Step 3: A Glowing Orb of Luclinite

The Glowing Orb of Luclinite is a raid-dropped item that serves as the "power source" for the scepter. It drops 6 per kill from several raid bosses across Luclin (enough for 6 raid members per boss kill).

Shei Vinitras is a two-phase fight in Akheva Ruins. The initial version must be killed first, which then spawns the real Shei Vinitras. She periodically spawns waves of Tatrua adds (Diabo, Thall, Tavuel, Va Tatrua) every ~5 minutes during combat, and her Xin trash mobs spawn additional adds when they get kills.

The Insanity Crawler in Akheva Ruins has a 2-hour despawn timer and spawns mind worm adds when it slays players. At 150k HP it is the easiest of the bosses. A zone-wide emote warns of its spawn: "You hear squealing voices of Centi echo through the dark hallways."

The Itraer Vius in Akheva Ruins is a 600k HP boss — a challenging fight but another source of orbs in the same zone.

Thought Horror Overfiend in The Deep is one of the toughest bosses on this list at 800k HP.

Grieg Veneficus in Grieg's End is a level 65 boss with 325k HP. On death, he spawns a lootable corpse version that carries his loot table. Grieg's End is accessible from Dawnshroud Peaks.

Servitor of Luclin in Grieg's End is a 120k HP boss — the easiest orb source alongside The Insanity Crawler.

The Ssraeshza Temple bosses (Xerkizh The Creator, High Priest of Ssraeshza, and Rhag`Zhezum) are high-end encounters that also drop orbs. Guilds clearing SSRA Temple for gear progression can collect orbs during these clears.

Step 4: Assembling the Key

With all components collected, the assembly is a two-step tradeskill combine process.

Combine 1: Unadorned Scepter of Shadows

▶
Place all 10 Lucid Shards into the Shadowed Scepter Frame and combine
▶

Open the Shadowed Scepter Frame as a container. Place all 10 unique Lucid Shards inside. Click Combine. The result is the Unadorned Scepter of Shadows.

Make sure you have exactly one of each shard code: Kel, Dat, Set, Raf, Vin, Dax, Lor, Tak, Ved, and Kelara.

Combine 2: The Scepter of Shadows

▶
Combine the Unadorned Scepter of Shadows with A Glowing Orb of Luclinite
▶

The Unadorned Scepter of Shadows is itself a container. Place A Glowing Orb of Luclinite inside and click Combine. The result is The Scepter of Shadows — the Key to Vex Thal.

Zone into Vex Thal from Umbral Plains
▶

The entrance to Vex Thal is a door in Umbral Plains (coordinates approximately -1912, -750). You must have The Scepter of Shadows in your inventory to open the door. Once keyed, you can enter Vex Thal freely as long as you carry the key.

Recommended Shard Farming Order

Because shards come from different zones with varying difficulty, here is a sensible progression:

  1. Dawnshroud Peaks (Lor + Kelara shards) — outdoor zone, accessible early; Sambata tribals and Rockhoppers for [Lor], Centurion Renardio and Intervallum Guards for [Kelara]
  2. The Grey / Scarlet Desert (Kel + Set shards) — outdoor zones; sun revenants and stone beasts in The Grey for [Kel], sun revenants and sun reavers in Scarlet Desert for [Set]
  3. Fungus Grove (Dat shard) — many Shik`nar types can drop this; one of the easier shards
  4. Acrylia Caverns (Ved shard) — runed grimlings and skullcrackers
  5. Maiden's Eye (Tak shard) — named Goranga spawns have very high drop rates but are rare spawns
  6. Akheva Ruins (Dax shard + Scepter Frame + Orb from Shei/Crawler/Itraer Vius) — combine shard farming with the Scepter Frame quest and boss kills
  7. The Deep (Raf shard + Orb from Thought Horror Overfiend) — difficult zone; farm the shard while working toward the boss
  8. Ssraeshza Temple (Vin shard + Orbs from Xerkizh/High Priest/Rhag`Zhezum) — requires SSRA key; farm Soriz mobs during temple clears

Tips & Notes

  • Track which shards each guild member has on a spreadsheet. Each shard code (Kel, Dat, Set, etc.) is unique, so you need exactly one of each.
  • Lucid Shards have a ~30-day lockout timer per character per loot source. This means if you loot a shard from a specific mob, that mob cannot drop another shard for you for approximately 30 days.
  • The Glowing Orb of Luclinite drops 6 per boss kill, so plan raid rotations to maximize keying speed. With nine different boss sources across Akheva Ruins, The Deep, Grieg's End, and Ssraeshza Temple, guilds can generate many orbs per week.
  • The Scepter Frame quest requires a Summoned: Wisp Stone for A shimmering presence, then an Essence Emerald for The Spirit of Akelha`Ra. Make sure to have extras in case of mistakes.
  • Shei Vinitras in Akheva Ruins is a two-phase fight that periodically spawns Tatrua add waves every ~5 minutes. She also has Xin trash mobs around her that spawn additional adds when they slay players.
  • Servitor of Luclin and The Insanity Crawler are the two easiest orb sources at 120k and 150k HP respectively, making them good early targets for guilds still gearing up.
  • Bards are useful for VT key farming across all zones. Improved Harmony helps with crowd control in dense areas like Akheva Ruins, and mez songs are critical for add management during boss fights like Shei Vinitras and the Thought Horror Overfiend.
