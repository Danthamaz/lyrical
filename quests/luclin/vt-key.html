Overview

Vex Thal is the pinnacle raid zone of Luclin. Gaining access requires assembling The Scepter of Shadows (the Key to Vex Thal) through a multi-step process involving items from across Luclin's endgame zones.

The quest has three main components:

  1. Shadowed Scepter Frame — obtained from The Spirit of Akelha`Ra in Akheva Ruins
  2. 10 Lucid Shards — dropped by trash mobs across 8 different Luclin zones
  3. A Glowing Orb of Luclinite — dropped by raid bosses (Shei Vinitras, Thought Horror Overfiend, The Insanity Crawler, or Grieg Veneficus)

The assembly is a two-step tradeskill combine: first you combine the Shadowed Scepter Frame with the 10 Lucid Shards to create the Unadorned Scepter of Shadows, then you combine that with the Glowing Orb of Luclinite to create The Scepter of Shadows. The Scepter must be in your inventory to zone into Vex Thal through the entrance in Umbral Plains.

Prerequisites

  • Ability to survive in Akheva Ruins, The Deep, Maiden's Eye, Dawnshroud Peaks, Scarlet Desert, Fungus Grove, and Ssraeshza Temple
  • A raid force for obtaining the Glowing Orb of Luclinite from any of the four boss targets
  • An Essence Emerald (summoned item) to turn in to A shimmering presence in Akheva Ruins for the Scepter Frame quest

Step 1: Shadowed Scepter Frame

The Shadowed Scepter Frame is a 3-slot container obtained from The Spirit of Akelha`Ra in Akheva Ruins. This is the container you use for the first combine.

The Spirit of Akelha`Ra Quest

▶
Find A shimmering presence in Akheva Ruins
▶

A shimmering presence is a questable NPC found within Akheva Ruins. Hail the presence and go through the dialogue about memories, the great temple, and ornate robes to hear the story of the scepter being shattered by a storm.

Turn in an Essence Emerald to spawn The Spirit of Akelha`Ra
▶

Give an Essence Emerald (a summoned item) to A shimmering presence. This causes the presence to transform, spawning The Spirit of Akelha`Ra (NPC type 179019, level 65).

Note: The Spirit of Akelha`Ra has a 30-minute despawn timer once spawned.

Speak with The Spirit of Akelha`Ra and turn in an Essence Emerald
▶

Hail the Spirit and follow the dialogue about her tormented prison, spirits, Atenha`Ra, and Vex Thal. She explains that her scepter was shattered in the storm and you need to reassemble it. She mentions you will need to "merge an Orb of Luclinite with a source of celestial power inside the scepter" and warns that "the emperor of the Shissar holds the power to manipulate such energy."

Give the Spirit another Essence Emerald (item 9963). She absorbs the energy, chants in Akhevan, and disappears, leaving behind the Shadowed Scepter Frame (item 17323) for you.

Step 2: The 10 Lucid Shards

There are 10 unique Lucid Shards, each dropped by specific trash mobs in different Luclin zones. Each shard has an internal code that makes it unique. Every shard is No Drop, and you need exactly one of each.

Shards drop at relatively low rates (typically 5-15%) from the listed mobs, with a shared lockout timer of approximately 30 days (2,629,800 seconds). This means each shard can only drop once per character per month from a given source.

Shard 1 [Kel] — Scarlet Desert

▶
Lucid Shard [Kel] — a sun revenant / a stone beast (Scarlet Desert)
▶

Dropped by a sun revenant (level 55, ~6% drop rate) and a stone beast (level 48, ~1.5% drop rate) in Scarlet Desert. The sun revenants are the better target with a higher drop chance. Sun revenants are undead and can see through invisibility.

Shard 2 [Dat] — Fungus Grove

▶
Lucid Shard [Dat] — Shik`nar mobs (Fungus Grove)
▶

Dropped by multiple Shik`nar types in Fungus Grove at ~15% drop rate:

  • Shik`nar Royal Guard
  • Shik`nar Warrior
  • Shik`nar Soldier
  • Shik`nar Herdmaster
  • Shik`nar Royal Advisor
  • Shik`nar Worker

Fungus Grove is accessible from Twilight Sea. Many different Shik`nar types can drop this shard, making it one of the easier shards to farm.

Shard 3 [Set] — Scarlet Desert / The Grey

▶
Lucid Shard [Set] — a sun revenant / a sun reaver
▶

Dropped by a sun revenant (~5-10% drop rate) and a sun reaver (~5%) in The Grey and Scarlet Desert. These undead creatures roam the open desert areas.

Shard 4 [Raf] — The Deep

▶
Lucid Shard [Raf] — a thought leech spiriter / a horror guard / Slavekeeper Xiox (The Deep)
▶

Dropped at ~15% from several named and trash mobs in The Deep:

  • a thought leech spiriter
  • a horror guard (level 50)
  • Slavekeeper Xiox (level 53)

The Deep is accessed from the Echo Caverns (Shadowhaven). It is populated by Thought Horrors and other mind-affecting creatures. This is a dangerous zone; bring a group or raid.

Shard 5 [Vin] — Ssraeshza Temple

▶
Lucid Shard [Vin] — A Poxed Soriz / A Soriz Corpse / A Soriz Drudge (Ssraeshza Temple)
▶

Dropped at ~7% by Soriz mobs in Ssraeshza Temple:

  • A Poxed Soriz (level 53)
  • A Soriz Corpse (level 51)
  • A Soriz Drudge (level 53)

These are trash mobs within Ssraeshza Temple. You need access to the temple (see the SSRA Key guide) to farm this shard.

Shard 6 [Dax] — Akheva Ruins

▶
Lucid Shard [Dax] — A shade golem and other trash (Akheva Ruins)
▶

Dropped at ~15% by A shade golem and other trash mobs within Akheva Ruins. The Akheva are powerful constructs that see through invisibility.

Shard 7 [Lor] — Tenebrous Mountains / Dawnshroud Peaks

▶
Lucid Shard [Lor] — Sambata Tribals / A Rockhopper / A Rockhopper Ravager
▶

Dropped at ~5% from various mobs in the Tenebrous Mountains and Dawnshroud Peaks areas:

  • Sambata Tribal Worker
  • Sambata Tribal Member
  • Sambata Tribal Hunter
  • Sambata Tribal Gatherer
  • A Rockhopper
  • A Rockhopper Ravager

These are relatively accessible outdoor zones. The Sambata tribals and Rockhoppers are common in the area, though the 5% drop rate means you may need to kill many of them.

Shard 8 [Tak] — Maiden's Eye

▶
Lucid Shard [Tak] — Goranga named mobs (Maiden's Eye)
▶

Dropped at 85-100% by named Goranga mobs in Maiden's Eye:

  • #a goranga battlemaster (level 53, ~85%)
  • #a goranga prophet (~85%)
  • #a goranga forager (~100%)
  • #a goranga savant (~90%)
  • #a goranga seer (~85%)

The Goranga named mobs are rare spawns in Maiden's Eye. The high drop rate per kill is offset by the rarity of the spawns themselves. These are named (rare spawn) mobs indicated by the # prefix.

Shard 9 [Ved] — Grimling Forest / Acrylia Caverns

▶
Lucid Shard [Ved] — a runed grimling / a grimling skullcracker
▶

Dropped by grimling mobs:

  • a runed grimling (~15% drop rate)
  • a grimling skullcracker (~5% drop rate)

Found in the Grimling Forest and potentially Acrylia Caverns areas. The runed grimlings are the better target.

▶
Lucid Shard [Kelara] — Centurion Renardio / An Intervallum Guard
▶

Dropped at ~15% by named mobs in Dawnshroud Peaks:

  • Centurion Renardio (level 50)
  • An Intervallum Guard (level 47, named spawn)

These are named spawns in the Dawnshroud Peaks zone.

Step 3: A Glowing Orb of Luclinite

The Glowing Orb of Luclinite is a raid-dropped item that serves as the "power source" for the scepter. It drops from four different raid bosses, with 6 orbs dropping per kill (enough for 6 raid members per boss kill).

Raid Boss Sources

▶
Obtain A Glowing Orb of Luclinite from any of the following bosses
▶

The Glowing Orb drops 6 per kill from these raid bosses:

Boss Zone Level HP
Shei Vinitras Akheva Ruins 63-65 650,000
Thought Horror Overfiend The Deep 63 800,000
The Insanity Crawler Akheva Ruins 63 150,000
Grieg Veneficus Grieg's End 65 325,000

Shei Vinitras is a two-phase fight in Akheva Ruins. The initial version (179032) must be killed first, which then spawns the real Shei Vinitras (179017). She periodically spawns waves of Tatrua adds (Diabo, Thall, Tavuel, Va Tatrua) every ~5 minutes during combat, and her Xin trash mobs spawn additional adds when they get kills.

Thought Horror Overfiend in The Deep is spawned through a scripted event involving a Thought Horror NPC (153004) that lectures players through five emotes about the fate of Shissar slaves, then transforms into Xilniov (153002), a named Thought Horror. The Overfiend itself has a 2.75-3.5 hour respawn timer and is one of the toughest bosses on this list at 800k HP.

The Insanity Crawler in Akheva Ruins has a 2-hour despawn timer and spawns mind worm adds when it slays players. At 150k HP it is the easiest of the four bosses. A zone-wide emote warns of its spawn: "You hear squealing voices of Centi echo through the dark hallways."

Grieg Veneficus in Grieg's End is a level 65 boss with 325k HP. On death, he spawns a lootable corpse version (163389) that carries his loot table. Grieg's End is accessible from Dawnshroud Peaks.

Step 4: Assembling the Key

With all components collected, the assembly is a two-step tradeskill combine process.

Combine 1: Unadorned Scepter of Shadows

▶
Place all 10 Lucid Shards into the Shadowed Scepter Frame and combine
▶

Open the Shadowed Scepter Frame (item 17323) as a container. Place all 10 unique Lucid Shards inside. Click Combine. The result is the Unadorned Scepter of Shadows (item 17324).

Make sure you have exactly one of each shard code: Kel, Dat, Set, Raf, Vin, Dax, Lor, Tak, Ved, and Kelara.

Combine 2: The Scepter of Shadows

▶
Combine the Unadorned Scepter of Shadows with A Glowing Orb of Luclinite
▶

The Unadorned Scepter of Shadows (item 17324) is itself a container (400 slot). Place A Glowing Orb of Luclinite (item 22196) inside and click Combine. The result is The Scepter of Shadows (item 22198) — the Key to Vex Thal.

Zone into Vex Thal from Umbral Plains
▶

The entrance to Vex Thal is a door in Umbral Plains (coordinates approximately -1912, -750). You must have The Scepter of Shadows in your inventory to open the door. Once keyed, you can enter Vex Thal freely as long as you carry the key.

Recommended Shard Farming Order

Because shards come from different zones with varying difficulty, here is a sensible progression:

  1. Tenebrous Mountains / Dawnshroud Peaks (Lor + Kelara shards) — outdoor zones, accessible early; Sambata tribals and Rockhoppers for [Lor], Centurion Renardio and Intervallum Guards for [Kelara]
  2. Scarlet Desert / The Grey (Kel + Set shards) — outdoor zones; sun revenants, sun reavers, and stone beasts
  3. Fungus Grove (Dat shard) — many Shik`nar types can drop this; one of the easier shards
  4. Grimling Forest / Acrylia (Ved shard) — runed grimlings and skullcrackers
  5. Maiden's Eye (Tak shard) — named Goranga spawns have very high drop rates but are rare spawns
  6. Akheva Ruins (Dax shard + Scepter Frame + Orb from Shei/Crawler) — combine shard farming with the Scepter Frame quest and boss kills
  7. The Deep (Raf shard + Orb from Thought Horror Overfiend) — difficult zone; farm the shard while working toward the boss
  8. Ssraeshza Temple (Vin shard) — requires SSRA key; farm Soriz mobs during temple clears

Tips & Notes

  • Track which shards each guild member has on a spreadsheet. Each shard code (Kel, Dat, Set, etc.) is unique, so you need exactly one of each.
  • Lucid Shards have a ~30-day lockout timer per character per loot source. This means if you loot a shard from a specific mob, that mob cannot drop another shard for you for approximately 30 days.
  • The Glowing Orb of Luclinite drops 6 per boss kill, so plan raid rotations to maximize keying speed. Shei Vinitras, THO, Insanity Crawler, and Grieg Veneficus all drop orbs.
  • The Scepter Frame quest requires an Essence Emerald at two steps — one for A shimmering presence, one for the Spirit herself. Make sure to have extras in case of mistakes.
  • The Emperor Ssraeshza encounter in the game data shows 8 room guards (Heriz the Malignant, Yasiz the Devourer, Zlakas the Slayer, Nilasz the Devourer, Skzik the Tormentor, Grziz the Tormentor, Slakiz the Malignant, Klazaz the Slayer) that respawn 2-2.5 minutes after death during the fight. There is also a Curse of Ssraeshza mechanic that triggers if the fight takes too long. While the Emperor is not directly part of the VT key quest (contrary to common belief), your guild will likely be clearing SSRA Temple anyway for gear progression.
  • Shei Vinitras in Akheva Ruins is a two-phase fight that periodically spawns Tatrua add waves every ~5 minutes. She also has Xin trash mobs around her that spawn additional adds when they slay players.
  • Bards are useful for VT key farming across all zones. Improved Harmony helps with crowd control in dense areas like Akheva Ruins, and mez songs are critical for add management during boss fights like Shei Vinitras and the Thought Horror Overfiend.
