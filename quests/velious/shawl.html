Overview

The Coldain Prayer Shawl is an eight-stage quest centered on the Coldain dwarves of Thurgadin. Starting with simple errands, the chain escalates through six tradeskills, NPC dialogue chains, open-world mob hunting, and culminates in an escort raid event on Iceclad.

Important: The 7th (Runed) Shawl can be handed in to Chamberlain Krystorf to spawn the Dain. If you join a Dain farm and use your shawl to spawn him, you may be awarded your choice of loot from the Dain. Some melee characters pursue the shawl quest strictly for this purpose, as the Dain's head is used to obtain a Belt of Dwarf Slaying.

1st — Burlap Coldain Prayer Shawl

Frost Giant Toes — NPC: Loremaster Borannin

▶

Reward: Burlap Coldain Prayer Shawl (AC 1, INT +1, WIS +1, CR +1)

Tradeskills: None — NPC turn-in only

Say "for the crown" to Loremaster Borannin
▶

Loremaster Borannin is in the Thurgadin library area.

Loremaster Borannin says, 'Those eager to prove their loyalty to the Dain may do so by completing tasks that I have been commissioned to assign. If you wish to begin your service, return to me with four toes of our enemy, the Kromrif.'

Collect 4 Frost Giant Toes and turn them in
▶

Frost Giant Toes drop from Kromrif (frost giants) in Eastern Wastes, Kael Drakkel, and Great Divide. Give all 4 to Loremaster Borannin.

Loremaster Borannin says, 'Well done! You have completed your first task in service of the Dain. Here is the burlap coldain prayer shawl as a token of your effort. Speak with me again if you wish to be assigned another task.'

Faction: Coldain +10, Dain +2, Kromzek -1, Kromrif -5.

2nd — Cloth Coldain Prayer Shawl

Preserved Kromrif Heads — NPC: Loremaster Borannin

▶

Reward: Cloth Coldain Prayer Shawl (AC 2, INT +2, WIS +2, CR +2)

Tradeskills: None — NPC turn-in only (container combine is no-fail)

Say "task" to Borannin → receive Preservationists Box
▶

Loremaster Borannin says, 'Every year the Dain replaces the trophies in his trophy room. The time of replacement is near and I have been asked to provide ten Kromrif heads preserved and ready for mounting. Take this box and fill it with ten Kromrif heads, combine them and return the box to me along with your burlap shawl.'

You receive a Preservationists Box (10-slot container).

Collect 10 Kromrif Heads and combine in the box
▶

Kromrif Heads drop from frost giants. Fill the Preservationists Box with 10 heads and combine to create Preserved Kromrif Heads (no-fail combine, destroys the box).

Turn in Preserved Kromrif Heads + Burlap Shawl → Cloth Shawl
▶

Give both the Preserved Kromrif Heads and your Burlap Coldain Prayer Shawl to Loremaster Borannin.

Faction: Coldain +10, Dain +2, Kromzek -1, Kromrif -5.

3rd — Woven Coldain Prayer Shawl

Cooking Chain — NPC: Mordin Frostcleaver

▶

Reward: Woven Coldain Prayer Shawl (AC 3, INT +3, WIS +3, CR +3). Also receive Seal of Thoridain (AC 3, INT +3, WIS +3, CR +3, HP +30 — ear slot).

Tradeskills: Baking 122 (4 recipes with trivials 26, 36, 51, 68, 82, 95, 122)

This is a long chain: you deliver four increasingly complex meals to four different Coldain NPCs, each time returning to Mordin Frostcleaver between deliveries.

Say "chore" to Borannin → receive Borannin's Seal
▶

Say "chore" to Loremaster Borannin. He gives you Borannin's Seal and tells you to visit Mordin Frostcleaver.

Give Borannin's Seal to Mordin → Meal #1: Tundrabear Sandwich for Guard Leif
▶

Give Borannin's Seal to Mordin Frostcleaver. He gives you a napkin and instructions to cook a Tundrabear Sandwich.

Cooking the Tundrabear Sandwich:

  1. Baking (trivial 26): Tundra Kodiak Meat + Jug of Sauces in an oven = Saucy Bearmeat
  2. Baking (trivial 36): Saucy Bearmeat + Loaf of Bread in a mixing bowl = Tundrabear Sandwich

Tundra Kodiak Meat drops from tundra kodiaks in Eastern Wastes and Great Divide. Jug of Sauces and bread from Thurgadin vendors.

Deliver sandwich + napkin to Guard Leif → return receipt to Mordin
▶

Give the napkin and Tundrabear Sandwich to Guard Leif. He returns a used napkin. Give the used napkin back to Mordin.

Meal #2: Frost Bunny Stew for Trita Coldheart
▶

Mordin gives you another napkin (for Trita Coldheart) and instructions for Frost Bunny Stew.

Cooking the Frost Bunny Stew:

  1. Baking (trivial 51): 2x Snow Bunny Meat + Jug of Sauces in a mixing bowl = Saucy Bunnymeat
  2. Baking (trivial 68): Saucy Bunnymeat + 2x Water Flask + Spices + Pot on a spit = Frost Bunny Stew

Snow Bunny Meat drops from snow bunnies in Eastern Wastes.

Deliver stew + napkin to Trita → return receipt to Mordin
▶

Give the napkin and Frost Bunny Stew to Trita Coldheart. Return the receipt to Mordin.

Meal #3: Ulthork Meat Pie for Lorekeeper Brita
▶

Mordin gives you a napkin for Lorekeeper Brita and instructions for Ulthork Meat Pie.

Cooking the Ulthork Meat Pie:

  1. Baking (trivial 82): 2x Ulthork Meat + Spices + Vinegar in a mixing bowl = Spicy Ulthork Meat
  2. Baking (trivial 95): Spicy Ulthork Meat + Clump of Dough + Pie Tin in an oven = Ulthork Meat Pie

Ulthork Meat drops from ulthorks on Iceclad Ocean and Cobalt Scar.

Deliver pie + napkin to Lorekeeper Brita → return receipt to Mordin
▶

Give the napkin and Ulthork Meat Pie to Lorekeeper Brita. Return the receipt to Mordin.

Meal #4: Snow Griffin Souffle for Grand Historian Thoridain
▶

Mordin gives you a napkin for Grand Historian Thoridain and instructions for Snow Griffin Souffle.

Cooking the Snow Griffin Souffle:

  1. Baking (trivial 122): 4x Snow Griffin Egg + Spices in an oven = Snow Griffin Souffle

Snow Griffin Eggs drop from snow griffins in Eastern Wastes and Western Wastes.

Deliver souffle + napkin to Grand Historian Thoridain → Seal of Thoridain
▶

Give the napkin and Snow Griffin Souffle to Grand Historian Thoridain. He gives you the Seal of Thoridain (AC 3, INT +3, WIS +3, CR +3, HP +30 — a great ear slot item).

Turn in Cloth Shawl + Seal of Thoridain to Borannin → Woven Shawl
▶

Give your Cloth Coldain Prayer Shawl and the Seal of Thoridain to Loremaster Borannin. The Seal is returned to you along with the Woven Coldain Prayer Shawl.

Faction: Coldain +10, Dain +2, Kromzek -1, Kromrif -5.

4th — Fur-Lined Coldain Prayer Shawl

Tanik Greskil Rescue — NPC: Frundle Frenkler

▶

Reward: Fur-Lined Coldain Prayer Shawl (AC 4, INT +4, WIS +4, CR +4)

Tradeskills: Pottery 122

Say "brewing" then "accept this mission" to Borannin
▶

Loremaster Borannin explains that Tanik Greskil, a Coldain spy, was sent to Kael Drakkel and hasn't returned. You need to create a Vial of Velium Vapors to revive him.

Get Velium Purifier from Frundle Frenkler
▶

Visit Frundle Frenkler in Thurgadin. You must be at least amiable faction. Say "velium vapors" to receive a Velium Purifier.

Craft the Vial of Velium Vapors (Pottery)
▶

Step 1 — Unfired Velium Vial (Pottery Wheel, trivial 122):

  • Small Brick of Velium x1
  • Crystalline Silk x3
  • Glass Shard x2

Result: Unfired Velium Vial

Step 2 — Fire in Kiln (no-fail):

  • Unfired Velium Vial + High Quality Firing Sheet

Result: Velium Vial

Step 3 — Velium Purifier combine (no-fail, destroys Purifier):

  • Velium Vial + 2x Small Piece of Velium in Velium Purifier

Result: Vial of Velium Vapors

Find Tanik Greskil in Eastern Wastes and give him the Vial
▶

Tanik Greskil spawns via proximity trigger in Eastern Wastes near the Kael zone-in area. Walk near his spawn point to trigger him. Give him the Vial of Velium Vapors.

He gives you Tanik's Note and despawns.

Turn in Woven Shawl + Tanik's Note to Borannin → Fur-Lined Shawl
▶

Give your Woven Coldain Prayer Shawl and Tanik's Note to Loremaster Borannin.

Faction: Coldain +20, Dain +5, Kromzek -2, Kromrif -10.

5th — Silk Coldain Prayer Shawl

Crystalline Silk — NPC: Rexx Frostweaver

▶

Reward: Silk Coldain Prayer Shawl (AC 5, CHA +5, INT +5, WIS +5, CR +5, FR +5)

Tradeskills: Tailoring 115 (sub-combine at 51)

Say "prayer shawl" to Rexx Frostweaver → Glowing Bile Soaker
▶

After receiving the 4th shawl, say "advance my reputation" to Borannin — he directs you to Rexx Frostweaver. Say "prayer shawl" to Rexx.

Rexx Frostweaver says, 'Gather five strands of crystalline silk thread and combine them in a sewing kit to make a crystalline silk fiber. Do this three times. Combine each with a dose of glowing worm bile in this bile soaker. Then combine five of those silk pieces with your fur-lined shawl in a sewing kit.'

You receive a Glowing Bile Soaker.

Craft 5x Glowing Crystalline Silk Pieces
▶

Sub-combine 1 — Crystalline Silk Fiber (Tailoring, trivial 51):

  • 5x Crystalline Silk Thread in a sewing kit

Result: Crystalline Silk Fiber. You need 15 threads total (3 fibers per soaker combine, 5 soaker combines).

Sub-combine 2 — Glowing Crystalline Silk Piece (Bile Soaker, no-fail):

  • 3x Crystalline Silk Fiber + Glowing Worm Bile in the Glowing Bile Soaker

Result: Glowing Crystalline Silk Piece. Repeat 5 times.

Crystalline Silk Thread drops from crystalline spiders in Crystal Caverns. Glowing Worm Bile drops from glowing shardwurms in Great Divide.

Total materials: 75 Crystalline Silk Threads + 5 Glowing Worm Bile.

Combine 5 silk pieces + Fur-Lined Shawl in sewing kit → Silk Shawl
▶

Final combine — Silk Coldain Prayer Shawl (Tailoring, trivial 115):

  • 5x Glowing Crystalline Silk Piece + Fur-Lined Coldain Prayer Shawl in a sewing kit

Result: Silk Coldain Prayer Shawl

On failure the silk pieces are destroyed but the shawl is returned.

Show Silk Shawl to Rexx → he directs you to Betti for the 6th
▶

Give the Silk Coldain Prayer Shawl to Rexx Frostweaver for validation — he returns it. Ask about being "worthy" and he tells you to speak with his wife Betti Frostweaver about the embroidered shawl.

6th — Embroidered Coldain Prayer Shawl

Sacred Coldain Thread — NPC: Betti Frostweaver

▶

Reward: Embroidered Coldain Prayer Shawl (AC 6, CHA +6, INT +6, STA +6, WIS +6, CR +6, FR +6)

Tradeskills: Tailoring 208 (sub-combine at 148)

Say "embroidered" to Betti Frostweaver → Embroidered Shawl Pattern
▶

Betti Frostweaver says, 'It will be an honor to assist you in fashioning an embroidered shawl. The process is quite involved but I'm confident in your abilities. First you will need to create a spool of sacred coldain thread.'

You receive an Embroidered Shawl Pattern. Say "sacred coldain thread" for the thread recipe.

Farm thread components from Velious creatures
▶

You need:

  • Woven Frost Giant Beard x1 — drops from frost giants
  • Siren Hair x2 — drops from sirens in Cobalt Scar and Western Wastes
  • Manticore Mane x2 — drops from manticores in Cobalt Scar
  • Drakkel Wolf Whisker x2 — drops from drakkel dire wolves in Great Divide and Eastern Wastes
Craft Spool of Sacred Coldain Thread (Tailoring, trivial 148)
▶

Combine in a sewing kit:

  • Woven Frost Giant Beard + 2x Siren Hair + 2x Manticore Mane + 2x Drakkel Wolf Whisker

Result: Spool of Sacred Coldain Thread

Craft Embroidered Shawl (Tailoring, trivial 208)
▶

Combine in a sewing kit:

  • Silk Coldain Prayer Shawl + Spool of Sacred Coldain Thread + Embroidered Shawl Pattern

Result: Embroidered Coldain Prayer Shawl

Trivial 208 is high. On failure the thread and pattern are destroyed, but the shawl is returned. Bring extra components.

Show Embroidered Shawl to Betti → Rune Pattern for 7th Shawl
▶

Give the Embroidered Coldain Prayer Shawl to Betti Frostweaver. She returns the shawl and gives you a Runed Prayer Shawl Pattern. She tells you to show the embroidered shawl to Gilthan Brittleblade.

7th — Runed Coldain Prayer Shawl

The Rune — Multi-Tradeskill Chain

▶

Reward: Runed Coldain Prayer Shawl (AC 7, CHA +7, INT +7, STA +7, WIS +7, CR +7, FR +7, PR +7, DR +2, Worn: Mana Preservation)

  • Pottery — 162
  • Fletching — 168
  • Brewing — 168
  • Jewelcrafting — 162
  • Tailoring — 162

This is the hardest crafting stage. It spans five tradeskills and requires showing intermediate products to NPCs between phases.

Show Embroidered Shawl to Gilthan Brittleblade → pottery instructions
▶

Give the Embroidered Coldain Prayer Shawl to Gilthan Brittleblade. He returns it and explains how to create the Blank Rune via pottery.

Gilthan Brittleblade says, 'So, you've been chosen to attempt the rune, eh? I must say, I'm skeptical that an outlander can accomplish this task. But, far be it from me to stand in the way of the Dain's wishes.'

Phase 1: Craft the Blank Rune (Pottery)
▶

Step 1 — Enchanted Velium Powder (Mortar and Pestle, no-fail):

  • Small Brick of Velium + Runed Sea Shell

Result: Enchanted Velium Powder. Runed Sea Shell drops from named mobs in Cobalt Scar and Siren's Grotto.

Step 2 — Unfired Rune (Pottery Wheel, trivial 162):

  • Enchanted Velium Powder + Water Flask

Result: Unfired Rune

Step 3 — Blank Rune (Kiln, no-fail):

  • Unfired Rune + Water Flask + High Quality Firing Sheet

Result: Blank Rune

Show Blank Rune to Trademaster Kroven → Prayer Text + Fletching instructions
▶

Give the Blank Rune to Trademaster Kroven. He returns the rune and gives you a Prayer Text. He also gives you a Fish Oil Extractor if you say "cutlassfish oil".

He explains how to craft a Burin (fletching), brew Sacred Velium Ink, and etch the rune.

Phase 2: Craft the Burin (Fletching, trivial 135)
▶

Combine in a fletching kit:

  • Swordfish Tooth — drops from swordfish in Iceclad Ocean and Cobalt Scar
  • Ulthork Tusks — drops from ulthorks in Iceclad Ocean
  • Molkor Hide — drops from molkors in Cobalt Scar

Result: Burin (reusable engraving tool)

Phase 2: Etch the Rune (Fletching, trivial 168)
▶

Combine in a fletching kit:

  • Burin + Blank Rune + Etched Rune Pattern

Result: Etched Rune + Burin returned

Phase 3: Brew Sacred Velium Ink (Brewing, trivials 135–168)
▶

Sub-combine 1 — Liquid Velium (Brewing, trivial 135):

  • Small Piece of Velium + Rubbing Alcohol in a brew barrel

Result: Liquid Velium

Sub-combine 2 — Cutlassfish Oil (Fish Oil Extractor, no-fail, destroys Extractor):

  • 3x Iceclad Cutlassfish in the Fish Oil Extractor

Result: Cutlassfish Oil. Fish from Iceclad Ocean.

Sub-combine 3 — Sacred Velium Ink (Brewing, trivial 168):

  • Liquid Velium + Royal Kromrif Blood + Cutlassfish Oil in a brew barrel

Result: Sacred Velium Ink

Royal Kromrif Blood drops from Klaggan Iceshard, Vorken Iceshard, and Kromzek sorcerers in Kael Drakkel.

Phase 3: Ink the Etched Rune (Fletching, trivial 168)
▶

Combine in a fletching kit:

  • Etched Rune + Sacred Velium Ink

Result: Velium Etched Rune

Show Velium Etched Rune to Talem Tucter → Casing Pattern
▶

Give the Velium Etched Rune to Talem Tucter. He returns the rune and gives you a Rune Casing Pattern with jewelcrafting instructions.

Phase 4: Encase the Rune (Jewelcrafting, trivials 152–162)
▶

Sub-combine 1 — Rune Casing (Jewelcrafting, trivial 152):

  • 2x Platinum Bar + Small Piece of Velium + Rune Casing Pattern in a jeweller's kit

Result: Rune Casing

Sub-combine 2 — Encased Velium Etched Rune (Jewelcrafting, trivial 162):

  • Velium Etched Rune + Rune Casing in a jeweller's kit

Result: Encased Velium Etched Rune

Phase 5: Final Assembly — Runed Shawl (Tailoring, trivial 162)
▶

Combine in a sewing kit:

  • Embroidered Coldain Prayer Shawl + Encased Velium Etched Rune + Runed Prayer Shawl Pattern

Result: Runed Coldain Prayer Shawl

On failure the rune and pattern are destroyed, but the shawl is returned.

Show Runed Shawl to Borannin → directs you to the Dain for the 8th
▶

Give the Runed Coldain Prayer Shawl to Loremaster Borannin.

Loremaster Borannin says, 'Incredible! Never before has an outlander been skilled and determined enough to craft our sacred rune. Show me the shawl once more and I'll help you seek audience with the Dain.'

He returns the shawl and tells you to show it to Chamberlain Krystorf in the Icewell Keep.

8th — Blessed Coldain Prayer Shawl

The Iceclad Escort — Smithing, Jewelcrafting, Raid Event

▶

Reward: Blessed Coldain Prayer Shawl (AC 8, all stats +8, HP/MANA +50, all resists, Mana Preservation, Improved Healing focus, light source)

  • Blacksmithing — 187 (7 armor pieces, trivials 176–187)
  • Jewelcrafting — 182 (7 gem imbues, trivials 178–182)
  • Raid event — escort General Bragmur across Iceclad

This final stage requires crafting a full suit of Royal Velium armor, then escorting a Coldain general across Iceclad while fighting off waves of frost giant attackers.

Note — Dain Farming: This first step spawns the Dain, who is a raid target. If you join a Dain farm and use your Runed Shawl to spawn him, you may be awarded your choice of loot. Some melee characters complete the shawl quest purely for this — the Dain's head can be turned in for a Belt of Dwarf Slaying. You do not need to continue to the 8th shawl if this is your goal.

Give Runed Shawl to Chamberlain Krystorf → audience with the Dain
▶

Give your Runed Coldain Prayer Shawl to Chamberlain Krystorf in the Icewell Keep (Thurgadin B). He spawns Dain Frostreaver IV and returns your shawl (now marked as approved by the Dain). You also receive an Empty Coldain Issue Kit if you don't have one.

Hand-in sequence with General Bragmur → escort event begins
▶

This is a raid event. Bring a group or raid force capable of handling multiple waves of 50+ level frost giants. The escort is started through a two-step hand-in:

Step 1: Give the Approved Issue Kit to General Bragmur on Iceclad Ocean. He emotes putting on the Royal Velium armor, returns the kit to you (you need it for the Avatar turn-in later), then despawns and a second version of himself spawns in his place.

Step 2: Give the Expedition Orders to the second General Bragmur. He says:

The final orders, follow me closely! I pray that the Kromrif have not learned of our plans to allign with the gnomes.

The walking escort version spawns and begins marching across the zone. You must keep the General alive through all encounters.

Give Runed Shawl + Approved Kit to the Avatar of Below → Blessed Shawl
▶

After the General reaches his destination, the Avatar of Below (an avatar of Brell Serilis) spawns at the final stop. He despawns after 30 minutes, so act quickly. Say "runed coldain prayer shawl" to him, then give him both your Runed Coldain Prayer Shawl and the Approved Issue Kit (which General Bragmur returned to you during the first hand-in).

The Avatar of Below blesses the shawl with the power of Brell Serilis.

You receive the Blessed Coldain Prayer Shawl.

Warning: This turn-in cannot be multiquested. You must be the person who did the entire escort event.
