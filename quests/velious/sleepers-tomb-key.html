Overview

Sleeper's Tomb is one of the most famous raid zones in EverQuest, home to the four Warders who guard Kerafyrm, the Sleeper. The zone entrance is a dragon statue in the far eastern reaches of Eastern Wastes. To enter the zone you must possess the Sleeper's Key, a No Drop key item that goes on your keyring.

The Sleeper's Key is obtained by speaking to Jaled Dar's Shade in Dragon Necropolis and turning in a dragon talisman. There are six different talismans that work, each dropped by a different First Brood dragon or Kael raid boss. You only need one.

Once inside, the Warders are the primary raid content. They drop Primal Velium weapons, resistance stones, level 60 Velious spells, Spell: Primal Avatar, and Ancient Prismatic gear. Killing all four Warders will awaken Kerafyrm, a permanent server-wide event.

Step-by-Step Guide

Obtaining the Sleeper's Key

▶
Obtain a Dragon Talisman (any one of six)
▶

You need one talisman from any of the following sources. Each talisman drops at 100% chance unless noted:

  • Klandicar's Talisman -- drops from Klandicar in Western Wastes
  • Sontalak's Talisman -- drops from Sontalak in Western Wastes
  • Zlandicar's Talisman -- drops from Zlandicar in Dragon Necropolis
  • Lendiniara's Talisman -- drops from Lendiniara the Keeper in Western Wastes
  • Yelinak's Talisman -- drops from Lord Yelinak in Skyshrine
  • Shard of Hsagra's Talisman -- drops from King Tormax, The Statue of Rallos Zek, and Derakor the Vindicator in Kael Drakkel, and from Velketor the Sorcerer in Velketor's Labyrinth (5-25% drop rate depending on NPC)

The first five talismans are also stat items (all-class, Lore, No Drop). The Shard of Hsagra's Talisman is a quest-only item with no stats. All talismans are consumed on turn-in.

Travel to Dragon Necropolis and find Jaled Dar's Shade
▶

Jaled Dar's Shade is a level 70 undead dragon in Dragon Necropolis. He periodically shouts zone-wide messages such as "Somebody is there, I can tell! Listen to me! We were WRONG, he must be awakened!" He is very powerful (3,000,000 HP) and should not be attacked.

Hail him and follow his dialogue about the talismans. He will explain that Sontalak, Lendiniara, Klandicar, Yelinak, and Zlandicar each have a talisman, and that a large piece from a dead First Brood dragon also works.

Turn in any one talisman to Jaled Dar's Shade
▶

Give Jaled Dar's Shade any one of the six talisman items listed above. He will say:

"Very well done. Here is how you unlock the entry. Seek out a great dragon statue in the Eastern Wastes, and use this key there."

You will receive the Sleeper's Key and 50,000 experience. The key is No Drop and Lore, and is automatically placed on your keyring.

Use the Sleeper's Key at the dragon statue in Eastern Wastes
▶

Travel to the far eastern edge of Eastern Wastes. The zone-in to Sleeper's Tomb is a dragon statue (the "Shrine Globe"). With the Sleeper's Key on your keyring, click the statue to zone into Sleeper's Tomb.

The Four Warders

All four Warders are level 70 Prismatic Dragons with 200,000 HP each. They belong to the Warders of The Claw faction and spawn in the lower chamber of Sleeper's Tomb. They respawn approximately every 19.7 days (1,700,000 seconds). Each has a distinct elemental weakness.

Warder Details

▶
Nanzata the Warder
▶

Nanzata the Warder -- 200,000 HP, level 70. Resists: MR 250, CR 100 (weak), DR 250, FR 300, PR 250. Nanzata is vulnerable to cold-based spells and has the highest fire resistance of any Warder. Loottable includes Smolder, Dragon Scaled Mask, and Red Resistance Stone.

Ventani the Warder
▶

Ventani the Warder -- 200,000 HP, level 70. Resists: MR 320, CR 250, DR 100 (weak), FR 250, PR 325. Ventani is vulnerable to disease-based spells and has the highest magic and poison resistance. Loottable includes Salindrite Dagger and Green Resistance Stone.

Tukaarak the Warder
▶

Tukaarak the Warder -- 200,000 HP, level 70. Resists: MR 250, CR 260, DR 250, FR 100 (weak), PR 250. Tukaarak is vulnerable to fire-based spells and otherwise has balanced resists. Loottable includes Mask of Tinkering, Blue Resistance Stone, and Dainty Gloves.

Hraashna the Warder
▶

Hraashna the Warder -- 200,000 HP, level 70. Resists: MR 80 (weak), CR 260, DR 235, FR 260, PR 235. Hraashna is vulnerable to magic-based spells and has the highest HP regeneration of any Warder (11,310 vs 10,560 for the others). Loottable includes Writ of Dragonkind, White Resistance Stone, and Clawed Guardian Bracer.

Warning: If all four Warders are killed, Kerafyrm the Sleeper will awaken. This is a permanent, server-wide event. Once triggered, the Warders are removed from the game forever and will never respawn. Coordinate with your server community before engaging the final Warder.

Warder Loot

All four Warders share a common loot pool of Primal Velium weapons (6 drops per kill at 6.25% chance each) in addition to their unique drops. They also drop Velious level 60 spells and have a chance at Spell: Primal Avatar.

Primal Velium Weapons (shared loot, all Warders)

▶
  • Primal Velium Battlehammer
  • Primal Velium Spear
  • Primal Velium Claidhmore
  • Primal Velium Brawl Stick
  • Primal Velium War Lance
  • Primal Velium Reinforced Bow
  • Primal Velium Battle Axe
  • Primal Velium Warsword

Ancient Prismatic Weapons (ST 2.0 loot, all Warders)

▶

Each Warder also drops 2 items at 100% chance from the Ancient Prismatic weapon pool, which includes items such as the Ancient Prismatic Bow, Ancient Prismatic Lance, and Ancient Prismatic Axe, along with the Eye of Vyskudra, Eye of Kildrukaun, and other eye items at lower drop rates.

Replacement Key (Nelef the Trader)

If you have lost your Sleeper's Key, a replacement can be obtained from Nelef the Trader, a halfling merchant located near the Sleeper's Tomb entrance in Eastern Wastes.

Getting a Replacement Key

▶
Speak to Nelef the Trader in Eastern Wastes
▶

Nelef the Trader is located near the dragon statue entrance to Sleeper's Tomb in Eastern Wastes. Hail him and ask about "prismatic power". He explains that a dragon destroyed the keys of some adventurers and gave them a trinket in return. He will trade an Essence item for a replacement Sleeper's Key.

Turn in an Essence item to receive a replacement Sleeper's Key
▶

Give Nelef any one of the following items to receive a new Sleeper's Key:

  • Essence Lens -- contains Lendiniara's essence
  • Essence Mace -- contains Zlandicar's essence
  • Essence Blade -- contains Sontalak's essence
  • Essence Pearl -- contains Klandicar's essence
  • Essence Ring -- contains Yelinak's essence

Tips & Notes

  • The easiest talisman to obtain is the Shard of Hsagra's Talisman, which drops from multiple Kael bosses including King Tormax and The Statue of Rallos Zek. The named talismans from open-world dragons like Klandicar and Sontalak are contested and harder to acquire.
  • The Sleeper's Key goes on your keyring automatically. You do not need to keep it in your inventory after first use.
  • Each Warder has one very low resistance -- focus your casters on that element. Nanzata is weak to cold, Ventani to disease, Tukaarak to fire, and Hraashna to magic.
  • Hraashna regenerates HP faster than the other three Warders (11,310 vs 10,560 HP regen). Maximize DPS on this fight to outpace her healing.
  • Warders respawn approximately every 19.7 days. Coordinate kills with your guild's schedule.
  • The Sleeper event is a one-time permanent server change. Killing all four Warders triggers the awakening of Kerafyrm, who will walk through Sleeper's Tomb and travel to Skyshrine. The Warders will never respawn. Coordinate with your server community before engaging the final Warder.
  • Primal Velium weapons and Ancient Prismatic gear from the Warders are among the best items in Velious. Warder kills are both key-quest progression and prime loot nights for your guild.
  • Jaled Dar's Shade also hints at the lore: "Kerafyrm, the best of us, what we should be. We imprisoned him in a timeless slumber aeons ago." He believes awakening the Sleeper is necessary.
