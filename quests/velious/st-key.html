Overview

The Sleeper's Key grants access to Sleeper's Tomb, located at a dragon statue in the Eastern Wastes. The key is obtained by turning in any one of six dragon talismans to Jaled Dar's Shade in Dragon Necropolis. Each talisman drops from a different First Brood dragon or Kael raid boss. You only need one talisman to complete the quest.

Jaled Dar's Shade will return any incorrect items given to him. Only the six talismans listed above are accepted.

Step-by-Step Guide

Obtaining the Sleeper's Key — NPC: Jaled Dar's Shade

▶

Reward: Sleeper's Key (No Drop — required to enter Sleeper's Tomb) + 50,000 experience

Tradeskills: None — NPC turn-in only

Obtain a Dragon Talisman (any one of six)
▶

You need one talisman from any of the following sources:

  • Shard of Hsagra's Talisman — drops from King Tormax, The Statue of Rallos Zek, and Derakor the Vindicator in Kael Drakkel, and from Velketor the Sorcerer in Velketor's Labyrinth
  • Klandicar's Talisman — drops from Klandicar in Western Wastes
  • Sontalak's Talisman — drops from Sontalak in Western Wastes
  • Zlandicar's Talisman — drops from Zlandicar in Dragon Necropolis
  • Lendiniara's Talisman — drops from Lendiniara the Keeper in Western Wastes
  • Yelinak's Talisman — drops from Lord Yelinak in Skyshrine

All talismans are consumed on turn-in. Only one is needed to complete the quest.

Travel to Dragon Necropolis and find Jaled Dar's Shade
▶

Jaled Dar's Shade is an undead dragon located in Dragon Necropolis. Hail him and follow his dialogue about the talismans. He will explain that several First Brood dragons each hold a talisman, and that a shard from a dead First Brood dragon also works.

Turn in any one talisman to Jaled Dar's Shade
▶

Give Jaled Dar's Shade any one of the six talisman items listed above. He will say:

"Very well done. Here is how you unlock the entry. Seek out a great dragon statue in the Eastern Wastes, and use this key there. Not very different from how you entered my old domain, Veeshan's Peak, now, is it? Haha, yes, I know many things, even dead."

You will receive the Sleeper's Key and 50,000 experience. The key is No Drop and Lore, and is automatically placed on your keyring.

Note: Jaled Dar's Shade will return any incorrect items given to him. Only the six talismans are accepted.

Use the Sleeper's Key at the dragon statue in Eastern Wastes
▶

Travel to the far eastern edge of Eastern Wastes. The zone-in to Sleeper's Tomb is a great dragon statue. With the Sleeper's Key on your keyring, click the statue to zone into Sleeper's Tomb.

Tips & Notes

  • The easiest talisman to obtain is the Shard of Hsagra's Talisman, which drops from multiple Kael bosses including King Tormax, The Statue of Rallos Zek, and Derakor the Vindicator. The named talismans from open-world dragons like Klandicar and Sontalak are contested and harder to acquire.
  • The Sleeper's Key goes on your keyring automatically. You do not need to keep it in your inventory after first use.
  • Jaled Dar's Shade will return any item he does not recognize. If you hand in the wrong item, you will get it back — no risk of losing items.
  • As Jaled Dar's Shade hints, the Sleeper's Tomb entrance works similarly to the Veeshan's Peak entrance: use a key item at a dragon statue to zone in.
  • Dragon Necropolis is accessed from the Cobalt Scar zone line. Be prepared for dangerous mobs on the way to Jaled Dar's Shade.
  • This quest is per-character. Each character who wants access to Sleeper's Tomb must obtain their own talisman and complete the turn-in individually.
