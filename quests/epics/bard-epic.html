Overview

The Singing Short Sword is the Bard's epic weapon and one of the most iconic items in EverQuest. The quest spans multiple continents and requires collecting two missing pages of the Maestro's Symphony (pages 24 and 25) and having the legendary instrument maker Forpar Fizfla in Steamfont Mountains construct a Mystical Lute. All four items are then given to Baldric Slezaf in the Dreadlands to receive the sword.

The quest involves a relay race across multiple zones, a ghost story in the Estate of Unrest, hunting drakes and wurms in Kunark, raiding classic dragons for scales, and triggering a special Trakanon event in Old Sebilis. Several steps can be done solo or with a small group, though the dragon scale and Trakanon events require raid-level help.

Phase 1: Maestro's Symphony Page 24 Top

Konia Swiftfoot in West Karana is hosting a relay running contest. Complete the relay and she rewards you with the Maestro's Symphony Page 24 Top.

The Relay Race

▶
Speak with Konia Swiftfoot in West Karana
▶

Hail Konia Swiftfoot in West Karana. She is hosting a relay running contest. Say "like to participate" and she tells you the prize is a silly old music sheet. Say "ready" and she hands you a Torch of Misty, telling you to run to Misty Thicket and hand it to Fajio Knejo.

Give the Torch of Misty to Fajio Knejo in Misty Thicket
▶

Run to Misty Thicket and find Fajio Knejo. Hand him the Torch of Misty. He tells you to "Run onward, my friend, to the far off Desert of Ro" and gives you a Torch of Ro to deliver to Andad Filla.

Give the Torch of Ro to Andad Filla in South Ro
▶

Travel to South Ro and hand the Torch of Ro to Andad Filla. He tells you to "Seek out Misty Tekcihta near the arena at Lake Rathe. Run like the wind!" and gives you a Torch of Rathe.

Give the Torch of Rathe to Misty Tekcihta in Lake Rathe
▶

Head to Lake Rathe near the arena area and hand the Torch of Rathe to Misty Tekcihta. She smiles, taps her foot, and hands you a Proof of Speed (the relay completion token).

Return the Proof of Speed to Konia Swiftfoot in West Karana
▶

Return to Konia Swiftfoot in West Karana and give her the Proof of Speed. She exclaims "Excellent! You are quite a runner" and rewards you with the Maestro's Symphony Page 24 Top. She says she decided to keep the other half because it has a wonderful signature, hinting it might be worth as much as "an instrument the great Mahlin used." (This is the clue for Phase 2.)

Bard tip: Use Selo's Accelerating Chorus to speed through the relay. The route is West Karana -> Misty Thicket -> South Ro -> Lake Rathe -> back to West Karana.

Phase 2: Maestro's Symphony Page 24 Bottom

The bottom half of Page 24 is obtained from Konia Swiftfoot by giving her Mahlin's Mystical Bongos. Getting the bongos involves a long sub-quest chain through the Estate of Unrest, Solusek's Eye, South Karana, and West Karana.

The Serra's Doll Quest Chain

▶
Speak with Serra in the Estate of Unrest
▶

Find the ghost child Serra in the Estate of Unrest. Hail her -- she says she lost [something] and cannot go home without her doll. Follow the dialogue: say "something", then "doll" or "yes", then "note". She gives you a Ripped Qeynos Bards Guild Flyer.

Give the flyer to Belious Naliedin in South Qeynos
▶

Take the flyer to Belious Naliedin at the Wind Spirit's Song bard guild in South Qeynos. Hand him the flyer -- he recognizes you are seeking Baenar Swiftsong. Say "audience" then "introduction". He asks for 50 platinum. Pay him 50 platinum (give it via the trade window) and he gives you a Letter of Introduction, telling you Baenar frequents a serene fountain in the southern Karanas.

Give the Letter of Introduction to Baenar Swiftsong in South Karana
▶

Travel to South Karana and find Baenar Swiftsong near the fountain. Give him the Letter of Introduction. He asks about your [request]. Follow his dialogue chain: say "request" -> "history" -> "realm" -> "gnome" -> "face" -> "ghouls" -> "seen a child's ghost". He tells you about Serra and a doll he was to bring her. Say "doll" and he gives you a Solusek Mining Company Invoice, telling you to give it to Marfen Binkdirple. He also mentions [Marfen] resides within Solusek's Eye.

Give the invoice to Marfen Binkdirple in Solusek's Eye
▶

Enter Solusek's Eye (Sol A) and find Marfen Binkdirple in the gnome mining colony. Give him the invoice. He says "'Bout time ye got yer lazy bones down here!" and explains Baenar had the doll custom-made for a special child, spending all he had. He hands you the Mechanical Doll.

Give the Mechanical Doll to Serra in the Estate of Unrest
▶

Return to Serra in the Estate of Unrest and hand her the Mechanical Doll. Overjoyed, she exclaims "Oh! You have the dolly! Thank you! Thank you! Thank you! I can go home now." She gives you a Note for Baenar, asking you to give it to her father. She then fades away.

Give the Note for Baenar to Baenar Swiftsong in South Karana
▶

Return to Baenar Swiftsong in South Karana and give him the note from Serra. He is deeply emotional, revealing that Serra was like a daughter to him and that he was her true father. He says Maligar's caravan passed by heading toward Qeynos and gives you a Note to Maligar.

Give the note to Maligar in West Karana; kill his Enraged Doppleganger
▶

Find Maligar in West Karana (his caravan roams the zone). Give him the Note to Maligar. He reads it and sneers: "I knew that already, you fool. It was by my hand she died!" He despawns and an Maligar's Enraged Doppleganger (level 45) spawns at his location and attacks you. Kill the doppleganger and loot Maligar's Head.

Bard tip: The doppleganger is level 45 and manageable solo for a well-equipped bard. Charm a nearby NPC if needed for extra damage.

Give Maligar's Head to Baenar Swiftsong; receive Mahlin's Mystical Bongos
▶

Return to Baenar Swiftsong in South Karana and give him Maligar's Head. He curses Maligar and, grief-stricken, vows never to play his instrument again in Serra's honor: "In honor of Serra, the daughter that was denied to me in spirit, I vow never to play this instrument again!" He gives you Mahlin's Mystical Bongos.

Give Mahlin's Mystical Bongos to Konia Swiftfoot for Page 24 Bottom
▶

Return to Konia Swiftfoot in West Karana and give her Mahlin's Mystical Bongos (the "instrument the great Mahlin used" she hinted about earlier). She rewards you with Maestro's Symphony Page 24 Bottom.

Phase 3: Maestro's Symphony Page 25

Kelkim Menkia in South Karana had her lute mangled by gnolls and needs new strings. Hail her, then say "rare" and "wurms and drakes" to learn she needs guts from a chromodrac, a red wurm, and a huge onyx drake. Bring her all three and she rewards you with Maestro's Symphony Page 25.

Gathering the Gut Strings

▶
Obtain a Chromodrac Gut from Eldrig the Old in Skyfire Mountains
▶

Chromodrac Gut drops from Eldrig the Old, a level 51 named drake in Skyfire Mountains. He is a drake-model NPC with 15,750 HP.

Bard tip: Eldrig can be soloed via charm kiting. Charm one of the nearby drakes and use it to whittle Eldrig down. Be cautious of charm breaks, as Eldrig hits hard.

Obtain a Red Wurm Gut from Nezekezena in Burning Woods
▶

Red Wurm Gut drops from Nezekezena, a level 47 named wurm in Burning Woods. She has 9,550 HP and can cast spells.

Bard tip: Nezekezena can be charm-kited using nearby drakes or wurms in the zone. She is easier than Eldrig but still dangerous to fight toe-to-toe.

Obtain an Onyx Drake Gut from Blackwing in Rathe Mountains
▶

Onyx Drake Gut drops from Blackwing, a level 35 named drake in Rathe Mountains. Blackwing is the easiest of the three gut-string mobs with 1,675 HP.

Bard tip: Blackwing is low enough level to be straightforward for any bard doing their epic. You can charm a nearby hill giant for quick work.

Give all three guts to Kelkim Menkia in South Karana
▶

Return to Kelkim Menkia in South Karana. Hand her the Chromodrac Gut, Red Wurm Gut, and Onyx Drake Gut simultaneously (all three in one trade window). She snatches them and immediately begins to restring her lute: "This is wonderful, I will be able to play my lute again soon!" She gives you an old sheet of music she found -- the Maestro's Symphony Page 25.

Phase 4: The Mystical Lute

The Mystical Lute is assembled in three stages by the eccentric gnome instrument maker Forpar Fizfla in Steamfont Mountains. Before he will speak to you, you must obtain a referral from Vedico Windwisper in Butcherblock Mountains.

Step A: Obtain Vedico's Note (Butcherblock / Ocean of Tears)

▶
Obtain the Alluring Horn from Quag Maelstrom in Ocean of Tears
▶

The Alluring Horn drops from Quag Maelstrom, a level 45 named cyclops in Ocean of Tears. He has 8,812 HP and spawns on Cyclops Island among the seafury cyclopes.

Bard tip: Charm a seafury cyclops and use it to fight Quag. Charmed seafury cyclopes hit very hard and can bring Quag down quickly. Watch your charm duration and be ready to re-charm or run if it drops.

Give the Alluring Horn to Vedico Windwisper in Butcherblock Mountains
▶

Find Vedico Windwisper in Butcherblock Mountains near the docks. She is complaining about [shoddy service] because a [giant cyclops] stole the horn she was playing on a boat from Freeport. Return the Alluring Horn to her and she gives you a Note to Forpar Fizfla, asking you to deliver it to her gnome friend and suggesting he can make instruments if you need one.

Step B: Mystical Lute Head (Forpar's First Task)

▶
Give the Note to Forpar Fizfla in Steamfont Mountains; receive his note
▶

Forpar Fizfla is in Steamfont Mountains. He is initially hostile to visitors ("Get out you pesky [race]!"). Hand him the note from Vedico. He warms up immediately and gives you Forpar's Note to Himself -- you must bring this back with the first set of components so he remembers who you are.

Say "mystical instrument" then "components". He explains he needs: the backbone of an ancient fishman, a strong tentacle from one of the long lost amalgyms, and a petrified skull of a lycanthrope.

Obtain a Kedge Backbone from Phinigel Autropos in Kedge Keep
▶

Kedge Backbone drops from Phinigel Autropos, the boss of Kedge Keep -- an underwater dungeon off the coast of Faydwer (approximately 50% drop rate). Bring Water Breathing potions or spells as the entire zone is underwater.

Bard tip: A bard can solo Phinigel using charm kiting with the fishmen in the zone, though this requires care given the underwater environment and Phinigel's AE spells.

Obtain a Petrified Werewolf Skull from a Drolvarg warlord in Karnor's Castle
▶

Petrified Werewolf Skull drops from a Drolvarg warlord (level 51) in Karnor's Castle on Kunark. The warlord is a named mob deep inside the castle.

Obtain an Amygdalan Tendril from an amygdalan in Plane of Fear
▶

Amygdalan Tendril drops from Amygdalan warriors and Amygdalan knights (level 48) in the Plane of Fear at approximately 7.5% drop rate. This item also doubles as a usable weapon (DMG 9 / DLY 25, STR +5, DEX +4, all classes). It may also be found on the Bazaar from other adventurers.

Give Forpar: his Note + Kedge Backbone + Petrified Werewolf Skull + Amygdalan Tendril
▶

Return to Forpar Fizfla in Steamfont and give him all four items simultaneously: Forpar's Note to Himself, Kedge Backbone, Petrified Werewolf Skull, and Amygdalan Tendril. He rambles around and works with small tools for ten minutes, then hands you the Mystical Lute Head. He asks if you want to gather the [next pieces] -- say "next pieces" to learn what he needs for the body.

Step C: Mystical Lute Body (Dragon Scales)

▶
Obtain White Dragon Scales from Lady Vox in Permafrost
▶

White Dragon Scales drop from Lady Vox in Permafrost Caverns, Gorenaire in Dreadlands, and Nortlav the Scalekeeper in Skyfire Mountains. Lady Vox is the classic source and a raid target. Coordinate with your guild as Vox is typically killed on a schedule.

Obtain Red Dragon Scales from Lord Nagafen in Nagafen's Lair
▶

Red Dragon Scales drop from Lord Nagafen in Nagafen's Lair (Sol B), Talendor in Skyfire Mountains, and Nortlav the Scalekeeper in Skyfire Mountains. Nagafen is the classic source and a raid target. Coordinate with your guild.

Note: Both dragon scale drops are used in multiple quests. Nortlav the Scalekeeper can drop both colors and is an alternative if classic dragons are contested. Plan ahead with your guild to secure them.

Obtain Metal Bits (tradeskill component or vendor)
▶

Metal Bits are a common tradeskill component available from various merchants or as drops. They are stackable and inexpensive -- check any Tinkering or smithing vendor.

Give Forpar: White Dragon Scales + Red Dragon Scales + Metal Bits
▶

Return to Forpar Fizfla and give him the White Dragon Scales, Red Dragon Scales, and Metal Bits simultaneously. He pulls out strange looking tools, works at his desk, and hands you the Mystical Lute Body, exclaiming "The body is done! Only one more piece [to go]!" Say "to go" and he tells you about needing the guts of a living dead poison dragon in the lands of Kunark for strings.

Step D: Undead Dragongut Strings (Old Sebilis Trakanon Event)

▶
Speak with An Undead Bard in Old Sebilis
▶

Enter Old Sebilis (requires Sebilite Key -- see the Seb Key guide). Find An Undead Bard inside the dungeon. He asks if you are a [minstrel]. Follow his dialogue chain exactly: say "minstrel" -> "trakanon" -> "instrument" -> "magical lute" -> "similar quest" -> "last request" -> "kill it for you". He asks you to hand him the Mystical Lute Body so he can hold an instrument one last time.

Important: You must have the Mystical Lute Body already crafted (from Step C) before this step.

Give the Mystical Lute Body to An Undead Bard; kill the spawned Trakanon
▶

Give the Mystical Lute Body to An Undead Bard. He says "I feel so powerful once again...", returns the Lute Body to you, and then turns hostile and attacks. Kill him, and upon his death a special version of Trakanon (level 65, 32,000 HP) spawns at his location and immediately aggros on you. This is a tough fight -- bring a raid group or strong group.

Kill the spawned Trakanon and loot the Undead Dragongut Strings from Trakanon's corpse.

Note: The Mystical Lute Body is returned to you before the fight begins, so you will not lose it. Make sure you have room in your inventory.

Give Forpar: Mystical Lute Head + Mystical Lute Body + Undead Dragongut Strings
▶

Return to Forpar Fizfla in Steamfont and give him the Mystical Lute Head, Mystical Lute Body, and Undead Dragongut Strings simultaneously. He assembles the final instrument and hands you the Mystical Lute. "I hope you find a good use for that! I bet you could play it from the mountaintops and the people below would hear you."

Phase 5: Final Turn-in to Baldric Slezaf

With all four components in hand, travel to the Dreadlands and find Baldric Slezaf to complete the epic.

Completing the Epic

▶
Travel to the Dreadlands and find Baldric Slezaf
▶

Baldric Slezaf is located in the Dreadlands. He has been searching for a place to hold a symphony but is missing two pages of music and needs a new lute. Hail him and follow his dialogue: say "symphony" -> "snags" -> "pages" -> "lute" -> "music" -> "repay". He pulls out a fantastic sword and says he would part with it if he could perform his symphony.

Give Baldric all four items: Page 24 Top + Page 24 Bottom + Page 25 + Mystical Lute
▶

Hand Baldric Slezaf all four items simultaneously:

  • Maestro's Symphony Page 24 Top
  • Maestro's Symphony Page 24 Bottom
  • Maestro's Symphony Page 25
  • Mystical Lute

He exclaims: "I will put on a symphony the likes of which the gods have never seen. Thank you so much. Please take this sword as a token of my gratitude."

You receive the Singing Short Sword and 350,000 experience. Congratulations -- your Bard Epic 1.0 is complete!

Important: You must be at least level 46 for the turn-in to work.

Bard-Specific Tips

  • Charm kiting Eldrig the Old: Eldrig is level 51 with 15,750 HP in Skyfire Mountains. Charm a nearby drake and use it to whittle him down. Keep mobile and be ready to re-charm on breaks.
  • Quag Maelstrom: Level 45, 8,812 HP in Ocean of Tears. Charm a seafury cyclops -- they deal tremendous damage and can bring Quag down fast. Have a re-charm attempt ready.
  • Phinigel Autropos in Kedge Keep: Bring a Water Breathing item or spell. You can charm the fishmen for assistance. Phinigel's AE stun can be disruptive -- keep distance and use terrain.
  • The Sebilis Trakanon: The spawned Trakanon is level 65 with 32,000 HP. This is the hardest fight in the quest. Bring a strong group or raid. Have your Mystical Lute Body ready before going to Sebilis.
  • Relay race speed: Keep Selo's Accelerating Chorus running during the torch relay for maximum efficiency. The route is West Karana -> Misty Thicket -> South Ro -> Lake Rathe -> West Karana.
  • Dragon scales: White Dragon Scales drop from Lady Vox, Gorenaire, or Nortlav the Scalekeeper. Red Dragon Scales drop from Lord Nagafen, Talendor, or Nortlav. These are raid drops used in multiple quests -- coordinate with your guild early. Nortlav in Skyfire can drop both colors.
  • All four items for final turn-in: You need to hand Baldric all four items in one trade window. Make sure you have Page 24 Top, Page 24 Bottom, Page 25, and the Mystical Lute all together before traveling to the Dreadlands.
