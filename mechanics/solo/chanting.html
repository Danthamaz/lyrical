Chanting (Solo Kiting)

Fear kiting and DoT kiting techniques for solo play

Overview

Chanting is the core solo kiting style for bards — layering targeted DoT songs on a single mob while keeping it snared or feared. Unlike PBAoE swarming, chanting works cleanly on one target at a time and avoids group XP penalties.

Songs

♫
Largo's Melodic Binding
String
Snare. Preferred kiting tool. Primary kite control song — keeping this up on your target is the foundation of any chanting rotation.
♫
Angstlich's Appalling Screech
Level 26 · Brass
PBAoE fear. Sends nearby mobs fleeing. Useful for breaking aggro or creating distance when a pull goes wrong or you need a moment to reposition.
♫
Chant of Flame
Level 10 · Singing
Fire DoT. First targeted damage-over-time. Core of early solo kiting — apply this and keep the mob snared while it ticks down.
♫
Chant of Frost
Level 34 · Singing
Cold DoT. Pairs with Chant of Flame for dual-element kiting. Some mobs resist one but not the other, making both songs essential for consistent damage across different zones.
