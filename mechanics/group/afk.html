AFK Barding (Group Support)

Songs to run on melody while supporting your group

Core Support Songs

♫
McVaxius' Berserker Crescendo
Level 58 · Brass
Attack speed and damage shield. Primary haste song for melee groups. Keep this running whenever Warsong is not available.
♫
Warsong of the Vah Shir
Brass
Overhaste. Best-in-slot haste when available. Significantly more damage than Crescendo alone — replace Crescendo with this whenever possible.
♫
Cantata of Soothing
Singing
Mana and health regen. Core support for caster and hybrid groups. Run this alongside your haste song to sustain the group through long pulls.
♫
Chorus of Soothing
Singing
Health regen only. Lighter option when the group only needs HP recovery. Use in place of Cantata when mana regeneration is not a concern.

Note: The Bard Epic proc applies 55% haste. When epic is proccing reliably, drop the haste song and replace with stats, resists, or utility.

Utility Songs

♫
Kazumi's Note of Preservation
Level 60 · Singing
Group invulnerability for 12 seconds. The group cannot cast while active. Apply movement speed before using so the group can reposition during the invulnerability window.
♫
Selo's Accelerating Chorus
Multiple Levels · Drums
Movement speed. Essential for travel, kiting setup, and positioning. Apply this before Kazumi's when using the invulnerability window to reposition the group.
