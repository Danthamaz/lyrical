General

ACArmor Class

CHACharisma — affects charm duration and vendor prices
ConConsider — checking a mob's level relative to yours
CRCorpse Run — retrieving your body after dying
DCDisconnected

LDLink Dead — disconnected from the server
LFGLooking For Group
LFMLooking For More (members)
ManaMagic resource used to cast spells
MedMeditate — sitting to regenerate mana
MemMemorize — scribing a spell to your spell bar
MobMobile Object — any NPC or monster in the game
MRMagic Resist
MTMain Tank — the player holding aggro
NamedA rare or boss mob with a unique name and loot table
NukeCast a direct damage spell
OOMOut Of Mana
OTOff Tank — secondary tank
PatPatrol — a mob that walks a route

PopSpawn — when a mob appears
PortTeleport — usually via druid or wizard spell
ProcProgrammed Random Occurrence — a weapon's triggered spell effect
PSTPlease Send Tell
PullBring a mob to the group's camp
RezResurrect — restoring a dead player with experience recovery
RootA spell that immobilizes a mob in place
SoWSpirit of Wolf — a run speed buff
STAStamina — affects hit point pool
STRStrength — affects melee damage and carry weight
TrainRunning mobs through other players, causing aggro on them
TwinkA low-level character wearing high-end gear from a main
WISWisdom — affects mana pool for WIS casters
WTBWant To Buy
WTSWant To Sell
WTTWant To Trade
XPExperience Points
ZEMZone Experience Modifier — a hidden multiplier on XP gained in a zone
ZoneA discrete area of the game world, or the act of crossing into one
