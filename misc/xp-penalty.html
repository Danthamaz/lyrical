PBAoE XP Penalty

How the experience penalty works for AoE damage at higher levels

Overview

When using PBAoE spells at higher levels, be aware of the experience penalty based on mob level. If a mob is level 40+ and you deal more than 50% of its HP with PBAoE, you receive a negative XP penalty.

Penalty Scaling

The penalty scales with level difference between you and the mob:

  • 0% penalty when mob is within 5 levels
  • Scales up to approximately 93% penalty at 20-level difference
  • At level 60, target mobs level 55+ to avoid significant penalties

Workaround

Workaround: Group with an enchanter who memblurs packs between pulls. Memblur resets the aggro state, removing the PBAoE XP penalty. This allows you to use PBAoE freely on mobs that would otherwise trigger the penalty.
