Flowing Thought

FT breakpoints, mana sustain thresholds, and Flowing Thought gear for bards

FT Breakpoints (Level 39 Charm)

  • 0 FT: ~20 min charm duration before running out of mana
  • 2 FT: ~2.5 hours charm duration
  • 4-5 FT: 2-5+ hours charm duration
  • 5+ FT: Standing perma-charm possible
  • 6 FT: Perma-charm with positive mana recovery
  • 10-12 FT: Endless charm swarm with mana for other spells (dirges)
