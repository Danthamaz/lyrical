AA Leveling Spots

Best zones for grinding alternate advancement at level 60

AA Zones

Fungus Grove

Shik'nar workers and foragers (level 53), mutants and bandits (49–53). 193% ZEM with excellent mob density. One of the top AA spots in the game for a level 60 bard.

Chardok

Indoor 50+ mobs. Risky but viable. Slow turn more manageable with string AE. Watch for patrollers and adds — the enclosed geometry makes unexpected trains a serious hazard.

Maiden's Eye

Target higher level mobs only. Avoid Umbrous Toilers — too low level, causes significant XP penalties. Stick to the Goranga and higher-tier spawns for clean AA rates.

Dragon Necropolis

Phase spiders. Requires 0 encumbrance to kite effectively — drop all heavy gear before entering. The open layout rewards clean kiting lines and punishes clutter.
