Best leveling path for bards on Project Quarm

Overview

Bards have multiple leveling strategies: swarming (AoE PBAoE kiting), charm kiting, fear kiting, and traditional grouping. This guide covers the best zones for each level range with ZEM (Zone Experience Modifier) values.

PBAoE XP Penalty (Level 55+)

When using PBAoE spells at higher levels, be aware of the experience penalty that kicks in based on mob level:

If a mob is level 40 or higher and you deal more than 50% of its HP with a PBAoE spell, you receive a negative XP penalty. The penalty scales with the level difference:

There is 0% penalty when the mob is within 5 levels of you, scaling up to approximately 93% penalty at a 20-level difference. At level 60, you need to target mobs that are level 55 or higher to avoid significant penalties.

Workaround: Group with an enchanter who memblurs packs between pulls. Memblur resets the mob's aggro state, which removes the PBAoE XP penalty for that mob.

Ideal Level 60 AA Spots

Fungus Grove

Shik'nar workers and foragers (level 53), mutants and bandits (49–53). One of the highest ZEM zones at 193% with excellent mob density.

Chardok

Indoor 50+ mobs. Risky but viable. Slow turn is more manageable with string AE. Watch for patrollers and adds.

Maiden's Eye

Target higher level mobs only. Avoid Umbrous Toilers as they are too low level and will cause significant XP penalties.

Dragon Necropolis

Phase spiders are an excellent target. Requires 0 encumbrance to kite effectively — drop all heavy gear before entering.
