Leveling Route

Best leveling path for bards on Project Quarm

Overview

Bards have multiple leveling strategies: swarming (AoE PBAoE kiting), charm kiting, fear kiting, and traditional grouping. This guide covers the best zones for each level range with ZEM (Zone Experience Modifier) values.

Starting Stats

Most Popular: Full STA (Stamina)

  • 100+ STA prevents stat penalties when fatigued (-10 STR/DEX/AGI) — best choice for a first-time character
  • Extra HP is useful for leveling and swarming
  • CHA is hardest to cap in Velious/Luclin without raid gear

Alternative (vaalkryst): All INT or CHA

  • Most other stats cap easily with gear and buffs
  • CHA provides +1% blur chance per 10 points
  • INT slightly increases mana pool
  • Mainly for min-maxing end-game builds

Note: Stats matter less at end-game due to gear, but STA gives immediate leveling benefits.

Race Selection

Half-Elf

  • Highest overall melee stats
  • Most popular choice

Wood Elf

  • Trades strength for agility

Human

  • Consistent stats, no night vision
  • People like it for the eyepatch model

Vah Shir

  • Runs the best (lol) and gets +10 safe fall
  • Gets access to the Vah Shir only cloak (Jharin Hero Cloak), but hard to acquire

Note: Race choice has minimal long-term impact due to gear.

Zeal & Technical Setup

Instrument Swapping

Turn on "Right-click to equip" in Zeal.

Lag Reduction

Add your Zeal folder to Windows Defender exclusions.

Swarming

Use "Slow Turn" to help with movement. Find this in ALT+O > Keybinds > Movement.

FPS

Cap your background FPS to keep the game smooth.
