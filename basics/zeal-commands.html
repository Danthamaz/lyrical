Getting Started

Zeal Commands

Complete reference for Zeal slash commands

General

/alarmSets an alarm timer. /alarm <seconds> — plays a sound after the specified time. Useful for tracking repops or buff durations.
/aspectratioSets a custom aspect ratio for the game window. /aspectratio <value> — e.g. /aspectratio 1.777 for 16:9.
/assistEnhanced assist command. /assist <name> or /assist group or /assist raid to assist the main assist.
/autofireToggles auto-fire for ranged attacks on or off.
/autoaaswitchAutomatically toggles AA experience on/off when you reach your AA goal. /autoaaswitch on|off.
/autoconsentAutomatically consents players when they request to drag your corpse. /autoconsent group|guild|raid on|off.
/autoraidinviteAutomatically accept raid invites. /autoraidinvite on|off.
/fovSets the field of view. /fov <value> — default is 45, higher values show more of the world.
/sitEnhanced sit command that works more reliably than the default.
/runSets run speed multiplier (client-side visual only, does not cheat). /run <value>.
/help zealLists all available Zeal commands in the chat window.
/zeal versionDisplays the currently installed Zeal version number.

Movement & Camera

/dampenlevDampens the camera bounce caused by levitate. /dampenlev on|off.
/followEnhanced follow command with better pathing. /follow on current target.
/zealcamEnables Zeal's enhanced camera system with smooth panning and zoom. /zealcam on|off.
/pandelaySets the delay before the camera starts panning when you turn. /pandelay <ms>.
/selfclickthruMakes your own character model click-through so you don't target yourself. /selfclickthru on|off.

Map & Navigation

/map on|offToggles the in-game map overlay on or off.
/map sizeSets the map size as a percentage of the screen. /map size <percent> — e.g. /map size 50.
/map markerPlaces a marker on the map at specified coordinates. /map marker <y> <x>.
/map zoomSets the map zoom level. /map zoom <value>.
/map backgroundSets the map background opacity. /map background <0-100>.
/map levelFilters the map to show only the specified level range. /map level <min> <max>.
/map ringAdds a distance ring around your position on the map. /map ring <distance>.
/map gridToggles a grid overlay on the map. /map grid on|off.
/map labelsToggles labels for points of interest. /map labels on|off.
/map poiManages points of interest on the map.
/map show_groupToggles display of group member positions on the map. /map show_group on|off.
/map show_raidToggles display of raid member positions on the map. /map show_raid on|off.
/map lineDraws a line on the map between two points. /map line <y1> <x1> <y2> <x2>.
/map gsay, /map rsayBroadcasts your current map marker or position to group or raid chat.

Targeting

/targetEnhanced target command. /target <name> with partial name matching and nearest-first priority.
/targetpreviousCycles back to the previous target in the target list.
/targetringToggles the target ring displayed under the targeted mob. /targetring on|off or /targetring size <value>.
/leftclickconLeft-clicking a mob will also consider it (show its level/con). /leftclickcon on|off.
/inspect targetInspects the currently targeted player.
/rtTargets the last player who sent you a tell (reply target).
/playerclickthruMakes other player models click-through to prevent accidental targeting. /playerclickthru on|off.

Songs & Spells

/melodyStarts a song melody (auto-twist). /melody <slot1> <slot2> ... — e.g. /melody 1 2 3 4 to twist the first four songs.
/stopsongImmediately stops the current song or melody.
/spellsetMemorizes a saved spell/song set. /spellset save <name> or /spellset load <name>.
/spelleffectsToggles spell particle effects on or off. /spelleffects on|off.
/useitemUses (clicks) an item in a specific inventory slot. /useitem <slot> — useful for macro-based clicky usage.
/timerDisplays a countdown timer on screen. /timer <seconds>.

Inventory & Items

/autoinventoryAutomatically places the item on your cursor into your inventory.
/autobankAutomatically deposits the item on your cursor into your bank.
/bandolierManages weapon and item swap sets. /bandolier save <name>, /bandolier load <name>, /bandolier list.
/protectProtects items from being accidentally sold, traded, or destroyed. /protect on|off.
/swapSwaps items between inventory slots. /swap <slot1> <slot2>.
/singleclickToggles single-click looting for items in loot window.
/locktogglebagslotLocks a bag slot so it won't auto-fill when using autoinventory.
/lootallLoots all items from the current corpse at once.
/lootctrlToggles whether Ctrl must be held to loot items. /lootctrl on|off.
/lootlastRe-opens the last looted corpse's loot window.
/linkallLinks all items from a container or corpse to chat.

Corpse Management

/corpsedragDrags the targeted corpse. Use to move corpses to a safe location for looting or resurrection.
/corpsedropDrops the corpse you are currently dragging.
/hidecorpseHides corpses from view. /hidecorpse all, /hidecorpse allbutgroup, /hidecorpse none, or /hidecorpse looted.
/tellconsentConsents a player via tell without being in the same zone. /tellconsent <name>.
/replyconsentConsents the last person who sent you a tell. Shortcut for quick consent during corpse runs.

Chat & Communication

/abbreviatedchatShortens chat channel names (e.g. "guild" becomes "g"). /abbreviatedchat on|off.
/classchatcolorsColors player names in chat by class. /classchatcolors on|off.
/classicclassesUses classic three-letter class abbreviations in UI. /classicclasses on|off.
/clearchatClears all text from the active chat window.
/timestampAdds timestamps to chat messages. /timestamp on|off.
/tellwindowsOpens tells in separate chat windows per player. /tellwindows on|off.
/optchatOpts in or out of custom chat channels. /optchat <channel> on|off.
/tell (tab completion)Pressing Tab while typing a /tell command auto-completes player names from recent tells and nearby players.
/logToggles logging of chat to a text file. /log on|off.
% replacementsUse in macros and chat: %n (your name), %h (target HP%), %loc (your location), %th (target HP value). Works in /gsay, /rsay, and other channels.

Raid & Group

/leadTransfers group or raid leadership to the targeted player. /lead <name>.
/raidmoveMoves a player between raid groups. /raidmove <name> <group#>.
/raidpromotePromotes a player to group leader within a raid. /raidpromote <name>.
/replyraidinviteReplies to a pending raid invite. /replyraidinvite accept|decline.
/sortgroupSorts your group window by name or role.
/raidbarsToggles raid health bars on or off for the raid window.
/surveySends a survey to raid or group. Members can respond with yes/no.
/consentmonks, /consentrogues, /consentclericsConsents all monks, rogues, or clerics in the raid for corpse dragging. Useful for quick raid corpse runs.

UI & Display

/nameplateControls nameplate display above characters and NPCs. Subcommands: /nameplate on|off, /nameplate colors, /nameplate font <size>, /nameplate x, /nameplate y.
/tagTagging system for labeling players or NPCs. /tag add <name> <label>, /tag remove <name>, /tag list. Tags display under nameplates.
/fcdFloating combat damage numbers. /fcd on|off to toggle damage numbers above mobs.
/showhelmToggles display of helmet models on your character. /showhelm on|off.
/shownamesToggles player and NPC names above their heads. /shownames on|off.
/reloadskinReloads the UI skin without restarting the client. Useful when testing UI modifications.
/uilockLocks all UI windows in place so they cannot be accidentally moved. /uilock on|off.
/tooltipallShows tooltips for all UI elements. /tooltipall on|off.
/tooltiptimerSets the delay before tooltips appear. /tooltiptimer <ms>.
/tickreverseReverses the tick counter to show time remaining instead of time elapsed.
/uierrorsToggles display of UI error messages in the chat window. /uierrors on|off.

Buffs & Stats

/buffsDisplays a detailed list of your active buffs with remaining duration.
/blueconAdjusts the shade of blue used for blue-con mobs. /bluecon <value>.
/cancelbuffCancels a specific buff by name. /cancelbuff <buff name>.
/clienthptickEnables client-side HP tick estimation for smoother health bar updates. /clienthptick on|off.
/clientmanatickEnables client-side mana tick estimation for smoother mana bar updates. /clientmanatick on|off.
/mystatsDisplays a detailed breakdown of your character's stats, resists, and modifiers.
/resetexpResets the session experience tracker to zero so you can measure XP from a fresh point.
/showlootlockoutsDisplays your current loot lockout timers for instanced content.

System & Output

/outputfileWrites data to a file. /outputfile inventory, /outputfile spellbook, /outputfile raid, /outputfile guild.
/pipePipes text output to an external program or file. /pipe <command>.
/pipedelaySets the delay between piped messages to avoid flooding. /pipedelay <ms>.
/pipeverboseToggles verbose output for pipe commands. /pipeverbose on|off.
/triggersManages text-triggered audio and visual alerts. Set up custom triggers for specific chat messages or game events.
