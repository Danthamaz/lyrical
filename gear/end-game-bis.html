Overview

This is the BiS set for bards focused on maximizing worn attack power. The majority of pieces come from Vex Thal. The total set provides 41% haste, 15 Flowing Thought, and 235 worn ATK (230 if opting for Umbral Vambraces over Mark of Shadows on the arm slot).
